## Setup

Place the Mars board. Set temperature to -30, oxygen to 0, and oceans to 0. Each player selects a corporation card (or draws 2 and picks 1). Deal 10 project cards; keep up to 4 by paying 3 Megacredits (the game currency) each. Set up the production tracks on each player board starting at 1 Megacredit.

---

## Gameplay

The game is played in Generations (rounds) until all three global parameters are maxed.

### Each Generation

1. **Research Phase:** Draw 4 project cards; buy any for 3 MC each or discard.
2. **Action Phase:** Players alternate taking 1-2 actions per turn (or pass) until all pass.
3. **Production Phase:** All players collect resources based on their production tracks.

**Actions available:**
- **Play a card:** Pay the card cost in MC; place it and apply its effects. Green (automated, stay in play), Blue (action cards, usable each generation), Red (events, one-time effects).
- **Standard projects:** Fixed-cost actions available to all: sell patents (1 MC), power plant (+1 energy production), asteroid (raise temperature), aquifer (place ocean tile), greenery (place greenery, raise oxygen), city (place city tile).
- **Card actions:** Use the action printed on a Blue card you own (once per generation, mark with a cube).
- **Convert plants:** 8 plants = place 1 greenery tile (+1 oxygen).
- **Convert heat:** 8 heat = raise temperature 1 step.

**Tile placement:** When placing tiles, award bonuses shown on covered spaces (MC, cards, titanium). Oceans placed on designated blue spaces. Greeneries raise oxygen 1 step. Cities score 1 VP per adjacent greenery at game end.

**Terraforming Rating (TR):** Each time you raise temperature, place an ocean, or raise oxygen, your TR increases by 1. TR counts as VP and generates 1 MC per generation.

---

## Winning

When temperature reaches +8, oxygen reaches 14%, and all 9 ocean tiles are placed, finish the current generation. Then score:

- Terraforming Rating (final TR)
- City tiles (1 VP per adjacent greenery)
- Greenery tiles (1 VP each, plus 1 per adjacent city tile — already counted above)
- Awards (funded mid-game, top 2 scorers in each category earn points)
- Milestones (claimed mid-game, 5 VP each)
- Cards with VP icons

Highest total score wins.

---

## Tips

- Fund Milestones and Awards early before competitors claim them.
- Engine-building through production tracks is more sustainable than one-time bonuses.
- Watch global parameter progression — the player who triggers the final parameter gets no bonus; plan timing carefully.
