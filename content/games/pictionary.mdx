## Setup

Divide into teams of 2–4 players. Place your team's token on Start. Shuffle the word cards and place them in the card box. Each team selects a first Picturist (the drawer). All teams keep their tokens on the board and move simultaneously when a team guesses correctly.

---

## How to Play

The active team draws a word card. The current Picturist looks at the word corresponding to the space color they are on:

- **Blue (All Play):** All teams compete simultaneously — first team to guess scores and moves.
- **Other colors:** Only the drawing team can guess.

The Picturist has **60 seconds** to draw and get their team to say the exact word on the card. Rules for drawing:
- No letters, numbers, symbols, or verbal sounds.
- No gestures.
- You may draw multiple images.

If the team guesses correctly, they roll the die and advance their token.

**Category squares (colors):** Person, Place or Animal (P), Object (O), Action (A), Difficult (D), All Play (AP).

---

## Winning

The first team to reach the FINISH square and successfully guess a word there wins.

---

## Tips

- For abstract words, think of concrete visual metaphors first.
- Draw arrows to indicate actions or relationships.
- Start with the most obvious aspect of the word and refine if your team is close but wrong.
