## Setup

Remove cards as specified for your player count. Shuffle the 16-card deck, set aside 1 card face-down, and deal 1 card to each player. Place favor tokens nearby. Cards range from Guard (1) to Princess (9).

---

## Card Reference

Guard (1, x5): Name a non-Guard card; if target holds it, they are eliminated.
Priest (2, x2): Look at another player hand.
Baron (3, x2): Compare hands; lower card is eliminated.
Handmaid (4, x2): Protected from effects until your next turn.
Prince (5, x2): Any player discards and draws a new card.
Chancellor (6, x2): Draw 2, keep 1, return 2 in any order.
King (7, x1): Trade hands with another player.
Countess (8, x1): Must play if held with King or Prince.
Princess (9, x1): Eliminated if discarded for any reason.

---

## Gameplay

Draw 1 card on your turn. Play 1 of your 2 cards and apply its effect. End your turn with 1 card. If you hold the Countess with the King or a Prince, you must play the Countess.

---

## Winning

A round ends when only one player remains or the deck is empty. The survivor or player holding the highest card wins the round and earns a favor token. First to the required token count wins (varies by player count: 6 tokens for 2 players down to 2 tokens for 6 players).

---

## Tips

- Track discards: with 16 cards, deduction eliminates impossible Guard guesses.
- Play Handmaid when you hold a high-value card you cannot afford to lose.
- Baron is strongest when you know your card outranks the target.
