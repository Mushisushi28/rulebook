## Setup

Each player receives a 40-card Dutch Blitz deck in their color (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow). Separate your deck into three piles:

1. **Blitz Pile:** Stack 10 cards face-down, then flip the top card face-up. This is the pile you must empty to win.
2. **Wood Pile:** Place 3 cards face-up in a row.
3. **Hand:** Hold the remaining 27 cards face-down.

Place the Wood Pile and Blitz Pile in front of you visible to everyone.

---

## Gameplay

On "go," all players play simultaneously with no turns. Speed is everything.

**Center Building Piles:** Any player may start a new center pile with any card numbered 1 of any color. Players may then build on any center pile in ascending order (1→2→3...→10). A pile of any color may receive a card of any color — color does NOT matter, only number and ascending sequence.

**Your plays:**
- You may play the top card of your Blitz Pile to a center pile if it fits.
- You may play the top card of your Wood Pile to a center pile or onto another Wood card (Wood piles build downward in alternating red/blue colors).
- You may cycle through your hand 3 cards at a time, looking for playable cards.
- When your Wood Pile has open spaces, refill from your Blitz Pile.

**Blitz!** When a player empties their Blitz Pile, they shout "Blitz!" The round ends immediately.

---

## Scoring

Count center pile cards you contributed: each card earns +1 point. Then count remaining cards in your Blitz Pile: each card costs -2 points. Tally net scores. First player or team to reach 75 points wins the game.

*Dutch Blitz is a traditional Pennsylvania Dutch game, currently published by Dutch Blitz Game Company.*
