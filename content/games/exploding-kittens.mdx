## Setup

Remove Exploding Kittens from the deck -- set aside one fewer than the number of players. Remove all Defuse cards and deal one to each player. Shuffle the remaining deck (including remaining Defuse cards) and deal 4 cards to each player. Insert the Exploding Kittens back into the deck and shuffle. Players start with a hand of 5 cards (4 dealt plus 1 Defuse).

---

## Card Types

**Exploding Kitten:** If you draw this and cannot Defuse it, you are eliminated. Out of the game.

**Defuse:** Play to neutralize an Exploding Kitten you just drew. Insert the Kitten back into the deck anywhere you like (secretly or taunting loudly).

**Skip:** End your turn without drawing a card.

**Attack:** End your turn without drawing, forcing the next player to take 2 turns in a row.

**Shuffle:** Shuffle the draw pile.

**See the Future:** Peek at the top 3 cards of the draw pile without showing others.

**Nope:** Cancel the action of any card except Exploding Kittens and Defuses (can also cancel a Nope).

**Favor:** Force another player to give you 1 card of their choice.

**Cat cards (various pairs):** Play matching pairs to steal a random card from another player.

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, play as many cards as you want (or none), then draw 1 card from the top of the deck. If you draw an Exploding Kitten and have a Defuse, play the Defuse and reinsert the Kitten. If you have no Defuse, you are eliminated.

Nope can be played at any time to cancel an action card. A second Nope cancels the first Nope.

---

## Winning

Last player not exploded wins.

---

## Tips

- Save Defuse cards -- once you run out you are living on borrowed time.
- Track where players reinsert Exploding Kittens after Defusing.
- Stack Skip and Attack to skip multiple draws in a row during tense end-game.
