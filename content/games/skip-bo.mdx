## Setup

Shuffle the 162-card deck (numbers 1–12 plus Skip-Bo wild cards). Deal each player a face-down stockpile:
- 2–4 players: 30 cards each
- 5–6 players: 20 cards each

Players look at only the top card of their stockpile. Deal 5 cards to each player's hand. Set the remaining deck as the draw pile. Create space for up to 4 center building piles.

---

## How to Play

On your turn, draw cards until you have 5 in hand (or draw 5 if your hand is empty). Then play cards in any order:

**Building piles (center):** Start a new pile with a 1 (or Skip-Bo wild) and build sequentially up to 12. Any player may build on any center pile.

**Discard piles:** You have 4 personal discard piles. Each turn, end your turn by discarding one card there face-up. You may play the top card of any discard pile on building piles.

**Skip-Bo cards:** Wild; count as any number you need.

Your stockpile top card is always available to play on building piles. When a center pile reaches 12, it is cleared.

---

## Winning

The first player to empty their entire stockpile wins.

---

## Tips

- Play your stockpile top card whenever possible — clearing it is the entire goal.
- Use Skip-Bo wilds to unlock stockpile sequences, not just as convenience plays.
- Organize discard piles strategically: keep similar numbers together for future plays.
