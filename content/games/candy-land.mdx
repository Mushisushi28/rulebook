## Setup

Unfold the board and place all pawns at the Start space. Shuffle the deck of colored cards. The youngest player goes first.

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, draw the top card from the deck. Move your pawn to the nearest space of the color shown on the card. If you draw a double-color card, advance to the second space of that color.

Special picture cards (Peanut Brittle House, Gumdrop Mountain, etc.) send you directly to that named location on the board -- which can be forward or backward.

If you land on a candy cane or licorice space, you may be stuck for a turn or have a special rule applied (varies by edition).

---

## Winning

The first player to reach or pass the Candy Castle at the end of the path wins.

---

## Tips

This is a pure luck game designed for very young children. The joy is in the colorful journey, not the strategy. If you draw a picture card that sends you all the way back to the start of the board, take it in stride -- it is part of the adventure.

Adults playing with young children can make it more engaging by narrating the locations visited ("Oh no, you are stuck in the Molasses Swamp!").
