## Setup

Shuffle the 15-card deck (3 copies each of Duke, Assassin, Captain, Ambassador, Contessa). Deal 2 cards face-down to each player as their influence. Each player begins with 2 coins.

---

## Actions

On your turn, take one action (truthfully or by bluffing):

**General (no card needed):**
- **Income:** Take 1 coin.
- **Foreign Aid:** Take 2 coins (Duke can block).
- **Coup:** Pay 7 coins, force another player to lose 1 influence. Cannot be blocked.

**Character actions (may be bluffed):**
- **Duke — Tax:** Take 3 coins. Blocks Foreign Aid.
- **Assassin — Assassinate:** Pay 3 coins, target loses 1 influence (Contessa blocks).
- **Captain — Steal:** Take 2 coins from target (Captain or Ambassador blocks).
- **Ambassador — Exchange:** Draw 2 cards from the deck, keep 2 total (reshuffle rest).
- **Contessa:** No action, but blocks Assassination.

**Challenging:** Any player may challenge a claimed action. The challenger calls the bluff. If the acting player reveals the card claimed, the challenger loses influence. If they were bluffing, they lose influence instead.

---

## Winning

When you lose both influence cards, you're eliminated. Last player with any influence wins.

---

## Tips

- Coup at 7 coins is unstoppable — collect quickly or spend before opponents Coup you.
- Bluff consistently — claim the same character repeatedly to appear credible.
- Challenge cautiously — a wrong challenge costs influence, which is life.
