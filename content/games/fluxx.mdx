## Setup

Shuffle the 84-card deck. Deal 3 cards to each player. Place the Basic Rule card in the center: Draw 1, Play 1. No other setup required.

---

## Card Types

**New Rules:** Change how many cards players draw or play, impose hand limits, or add other ongoing effects. A new rule replaces the old rule of the same type.

**Actions:** One-time effects: draw extra cards, steal cards, reshuffle the deck, etc. Discard after use.

**Keepers:** Play face-up in front of you. Required to meet Goals.

**Goals:** Replace the current Goal. The new Goal defines the current win condition (usually holding specific Keeper combinations).

**Creepers:** Automatically attach to you and usually prevent winning unless the Goal specifies them.

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, follow the current rules: draw the specified number of cards, then play the specified number. Playing a New Rule card changes the rules immediately (including for the rest of your turn). Playing a Goal card replaces the old Goal and is checked immediately -- if you now win, the game ends.

There is no turn order enforcement beyond draw and play counts. If you run out of cards mid-turn, draw more per the current rule. If the deck runs out, reshuffle the discard pile.

---

## Winning

Meet the current Goal (hold the specified Keepers, or whatever the Goal demands) at any point. Goals can shift every turn, so the win condition is always in flux.

---

## Tips

- Hoard Keepers that appear in multiple Goals; they give you flexibility.
- Watch opponents Keepers when playing Goals -- try to set Goals only you can currently meet.
- Inflation and hand-limit rules can dramatically slow opponents down.
