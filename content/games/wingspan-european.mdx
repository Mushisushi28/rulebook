## Overview

The European Expansion adds 81 new bird cards featuring birds native to Europe, a new end-of-round goal board with alternative scoring tiles, and the pink-power tray mechanic. It does not change the core rules of Wingspan but significantly increases deck variety and strategic options.

---

## New Components

**81 European bird cards:** Many have pink powers (see below) and unique interactions with food types and egg limits uncommon in the base game.

**New end-of-round goal board:** Flip side of the base goals board; introduces goals tied to habitats, specific food types, and column-based scoring rather than row-based.

**Goal tiles:** Two variants of scoring for each end-of-round goal — one variant scores top 1-2 players (competitive), the other scores all players who meet thresholds (cooperative scoring style).

---

## Pink Powers

Pink powers are a new bird ability color (in addition to white, brown, and gold) that activate during other players turns — specifically when an opponent uses a specific habitat. Pink-power birds activate on your opponent taking that habitat action, triggering effects like gaining food, laying eggs, or drawing cards for free.

This creates passive engine components that reward building a wide habitat presence.

---

## Combining with Base Game

Shuffle European bird cards into the base deck or keep them separate and deal from both decks simultaneously. All other setup rules remain identical. Mix end-of-round goals from both boards as desired.

---

## Winning

Same as base Wingspan: after 4 rounds, score birds, end-of-round goal tokens, bonus cards, eggs, cached food, and tucked cards. Highest total wins.
