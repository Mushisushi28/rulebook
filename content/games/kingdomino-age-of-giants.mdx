## Overview

Kingdomino: Age of Giants requires the base Kingdomino game to play. It adds three new modules that can be combined freely.

---

## Module 1: Giants

24 Giant meeples and 12 Giant Footprint tokens are added to the game. When a tile marked with a Giant symbol is chosen, the player who takes it must also place a Giant meeple on any terrain type that has at least one crown.

At the end of the game, each terrain type with a Giant meeple on it scores 0 points (the Giant cancels all crowns in that terrain). The Giant meeple must be placed immediately and cannot be moved later.

**Strategy:** Giants are both a threat (played on your own kingdoms by opponents) and a tool (play them on terrain you do not care about to neutralize their impact, or use Giant Footprint tokens to push Giants to opponents).

---

## Module 2: Giant Quests

Shuffle the Quest cards and reveal a set number based on player count. Quests are shared objectives that all players compete for. Examples: "Player with the largest wheat territory earns 7 bonus points." Quests are scored at end of game alongside normal scoring.

---

## Module 3: Fifth Player

Adds full components for a 5th player in the standard 5x5 grid format, including a new starting tile and matching colored pieces.

---

## Setup

Combine base Kingdomino tiles with expansion components. Set up per the base game rules and add chosen modules per their setup instructions.

---

## Winning

Standard Kingdomino scoring applies. Add Giant penalties and Quest bonuses on top. Most points wins.
