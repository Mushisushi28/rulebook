## Overview

Wingspan Asia introduces 183 new bird cards featuring Asian species, new bonus cards, new end-of-round goals, and two new play modes. It can be combined with the base Wingspan game or played standalone.

---

## Standalone Setup (Duet Mode — 2 Players)

Each player chooses a habitat board side. Set out the shared Duet map board alongside individual player mats. Shuffle the Asia bird deck (or combine with base). Deal each player 5 birds and 5 food tokens, keep 4 of each. Standard Wingspan setup otherwise.

**Duet map:** A shared 5x5 grid where players place tokens by taking specific actions. After each round, score the Duet map based on pattern goals.

---

## Core Rules (Same as Wingspan)

- 4 rounds, decreasing action count (8, 7, 6, 5 actions per round).
- Three habitats: Forest (gain food), Grassland (lay eggs), Wetland (draw cards).
- Activate habitat to trigger all birds in that row from right to left.
- Birds require specific food combinations to play. Eggs are used as currency.
- Powers: white (once, when played), pink (each activation), brown (opponent activates them too).

---

## Flock Mode (3-7 Players)

Flock mode adds a shared bird feeder that cycles between players, allowing up to 7 people to play simultaneously without turn order bottlenecks. Each player still has individual habitats, but food and some card draws are contested from shared pools.

---

## Winning

After 4 rounds, score:
- Birds (face value + bonus powers).
- End-of-round goal tokens.
- Bonus cards fulfilled.
- Eggs on birds, cached food, cards tucked.
- Duet map tokens (Duet mode only).

Highest total wins.
