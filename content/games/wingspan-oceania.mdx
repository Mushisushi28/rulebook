## Overview

Wingspan: Oceania Expansion requires the base Wingspan game to play. It is designed to be mixed into the base game and previous expansions or played as a standalone Oceania-only session.

---

## New Content

**95 new bird cards** from Australasia, New Zealand, and surrounding island nations. Birds include many parrots, cockatoos, seabirds, and unique megafauna like the cassowary.

**Nectar** is a new food token (pink flower). Several Oceania birds require nectar to play. Nectar is gained from bird powers and is special in two ways:
- Nectar can substitute for any other food type when paying to play a bird.
- At end of each round, all unspent nectar is discarded. It does not carry between rounds.

**New player boards** (2 included) feature different habitat bonuses, replacing one of the two original board layouts. The new boards offer more food flexibility and end-of-round actions tied to the number of birds played in each habitat.

---

## Integration

When playing with Oceania cards mixed in, all rules remain the same. Nectar tokens are simply added to the food supply. Birds that require nectar are clearly marked.

**Oceania-only play:** Use only the 95 Oceania birds plus base game rules. Shuffle and deal normally.

---

## Winning

Standard Wingspan scoring: birds, bonus cards, end-of-round goals, eggs, cached food, tucked cards. Nectar left in habitats at game end is worth points on the new player boards.

---

## Tips

- Nectar is powerful because it is flexible, but its volatility (discarded each round) means you must spend it each round.
- The new boards reward horizontal spread (many different habitats) rather than vertical depth in one habitat.
