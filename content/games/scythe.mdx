## Setup

Place the hex board. Each player receives a faction mat (asymmetric starting position, units, and abilities) and a player mat (unique action order). Place your mechs, workers, and structures in your home territory. Set coins, power, popularity, and combat cards per faction. Shuffle and deal encounter and objective cards.

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, choose a section on your player mat you did not choose last turn (move the pawn). Each section has a **top action** and a **bottom action** — you may do either, both, or neither, but must choose in order (top before bottom).

**Top actions:**
- **Move** — move units (workers, character, mechs) across hexes. Character/mechs can carry workers. Moving into enemy territory may trigger combat.
- **Bolster** — gain Power and/or Combat Cards.
- **Trade** — gain 2 resources of any type.
- **Produce** — workers on resource hexes generate that resource (oil, food, metal, wood).

**Bottom actions (cost resources):**
- **Upgrade** — remove a cost cubit from a top action and a bonus from a bottom action, improving both.
- **Deploy** — place a Mech on a worker-occupied hex, unlocking a faction-specific Mech ability.
- **Build** — place a structure on a worker hex, providing ongoing bonuses.
- **Enlist** — unlock one-time and recurring bonuses from the Recruit track.

**Combat:** When your unit enters an enemy-occupied hex, combat occurs. Each player secretly bids power (0 to their current power) and optionally plays combat cards. Highest total wins; ties go to attacker. Loser retreats to home territory.

**Encounters:** When your character enters an encounter hex, draw an encounter card and choose one of three options (resource, permanent bonus, or unique effect).

---

## Winning

The game ends immediately when any player places their 6th Star (achievement token). Stars are earned for: completing objectives, winning combat, maxing popularity/power, fully upgrading, deploying all mechs, building all structures, enlisting all recruits.

Score: Coins in hand + (territory controlled x multiplier based on Popularity) + (resources controlled x multiplier) + (structures built x multiplier) + Stars earned x coin value. Highest coins wins.

---

## Tips

- The player mat determines your efficiency — some mats pair bottom actions cheaply.
- Popularity affects your end-game score multiplier more than stars.
- Blocking tunnel hexes controls movement across the map.
