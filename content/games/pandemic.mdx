## Setup

Place the board. Shuffle the **City cards** and deal each player a hand (2 players: 4 cards each; 3 players: 3 cards; 4 players: 2 cards). Remove **Event cards** from the Player deck, add **Epidemic cards** (5 for standard difficulty), shuffle, split the deck into equal piles, place one Epidemic into each pile, reassemble, and set aside. This is the Player Draw Pile.

Each player randomly receives a **Role card** and the corresponding color pawn — place pawns on Atlanta.

**Infect the board:** Shuffle the Infection deck. Draw 3 cities — add 3 disease cubes each. Draw 3 more — add 2 cubes each. Draw 3 more — add 1 cube each. Return all 9 cards to the Infection Discard pile.

Place **2 research stations** in Atlanta (or just 1, per the print). Place disease cubes in their supply trays.

---

## Roles

Each role has a unique ability:
- **Medic:** Remove all cubes of one color when treating, not just 1.
- **Scientist:** Discover a cure with only 4 matching cards (not 5).
- **Researcher:** May give any City card to another player in the same city, regardless of whose city it is.
- **Operations Expert:** Build a research station without discarding a City card; once per turn, move from a research station to any city by discarding any City card.
- **Quarantine Specialist:** Prevent cube placement and outbreaks in the city and all adjacent cities.
- **Dispatcher:** Move other players' pawns as if they were your own (with their permission).
- **Contingency Planner:** Take any Event card from the discard and store it; once used, it is removed from the game.

---

## Turn Structure

On your turn, perform actions in this order:

### 1. Take 4 Actions

You have exactly **4 actions** per turn. Actions include:

**Movement:**
- **Drive/Ferry** — Move to a city connected to yours on the board.
- **Direct Flight** — Discard a City card matching your destination to fly there.
- **Charter Flight** — Discard the City card matching your **current** city to fly to any city.
- **Shuttle Flight** — Move from any city with a research station to any other city with a research station.

**In-city:**
- **Build a Research Station** — Discard the City card matching your current city to build there. If all 6 stations are placed, remove one from the board.
- **Treat Disease** — Remove 1 disease cube from your current city (or all cubes if you are the Medic, or all cubes of a cured disease for any player).
- **Share Knowledge** — Give or take a City card matching the current city from/to another player in the same city.
- **Discover a Cure** — At a research station, discard 5 City cards of the same color to cure that disease. (Scientist: 4 cards.)

### 2. Draw 2 Player Cards

Draw 2 cards from the Player Draw Pile and add them to your hand. If you exceed **7 cards**, immediately discard down to 7.

**Drawing an Epidemic card:**
1. **Increase:** Move the Infection Rate marker forward 1 space.
2. **Infect:** Draw the **bottom** card of the Infection Deck. Place 3 cubes of the matching color in that city (outbreak rules apply if already at 3).
3. **Intensify:** Shuffle the Infection Discard pile and place it on **top** of the Infection Deck.

### 3. Infect Cities

Draw cards equal to the current **Infection Rate** (shown on the track; starts at 2). For each city drawn, add 1 cube of the matching color.

---

## Outbreaks

If a city already has **3 cubes** of a color and would receive another, an **outbreak** occurs:
- Do not add a cube to the triggering city.
- Add 1 cube of that color to every city connected to it.
- Connected cities that also reach 3 cubes **chain-outbreak**, but each city can only outbreak once per chain.
- Move the Outbreak marker forward 1 space.

---

## Eradication

When a disease is cured **and** all its cubes are removed from the board, it is **eradicated**. Flip its cure marker to the eradicated side. No more cubes of that color are placed (even during epidemics).

---

## Winning

The team wins immediately when **all 4 diseases are cured** (they do not need to be eradicated).

---

## Losing

The team loses if:
- The **Outbreak marker** reaches position 8.
- Any disease **cube supply** runs out when you need to place a cube.
- You cannot draw **2 Player cards** (deck is empty).
