## Setup

Choose an Ancient One (each has unique doom and awakening effects). Set up the Arkham board with location cards, gate markers, monster tokens, and clue tokens per the scenario setup. Each investigator chooses a character with unique stats, starting items, and special ability. Shuffle monster cup, gate cards, and Mythos deck as instructed.

---

## Investigator Stats

Each investigator has paired stats (Speed/Sneak, Fight/Will, Lore/Luck) and a Sanity/Stamina track. Moving the dial on each pair trades one stat for the other. Sanity represents mental health; Stamina represents physical health. Reaching 0 in either means devourment.

---

## Turn Structure

**Upkeep:** Refresh exhausted items, pay retainer cards, make Blessing/Curse checks.

**Movement:** Move your investigator through the city (streets and locations) up to your Speed value. Some locations have inner rooms accessible from the street location.

**Encounters:** If in a location with no gate or monster, draw an encounter card for that location.

**Other Worlds:** Investigators sucked into gates travel through two Other World spaces before returning to Arkham (and potentially sealing the gate).

**Mythos Phase:** Draw a Mythos card. Open a new gate (place a gate token; any investigator there is pulled in). Add clue tokens. Advance monster surge or Doom track. Move monsters per their movement symbols.

---

## Gates and Sealing

Closing a gate requires an investigator to return from the Other World matching the gate and pass a Lore or Fight check. Sealing a gate (permanent) requires spending 5 Clue tokens during the closing check. Sealed gates cannot reopen.

---

## Winning

Investigators win by sealing 6 gates (or closing all open gates with enough trophy trophies). They lose if the Doom track fills (Ancient One awakens) or all investigators are devoured.

**Ancient One Combat:** If the Ancient One awakens, investigators fight it in a final combat round-by-round until all investigators are devoured (lose) or a miracle condition is met.

---

## Tips

- Seal gates rather than just close them; closed gates can reopen.
- Clue tokens are your most precious resource -- hoard them for sealing.
- Fight monsters only when necessary; bypassing on your way to a gate saves precious actions.
