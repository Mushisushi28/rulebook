## Setup

Shuffle the market cards and set up the dungeon with modular tile boards. Each player begins with an identical starter deck of 10 cards (burgle cards, stumbles, and sidesteps). Place dragon cubes and player Clank cubes in the bag. Set the Dragon Rage track to its starting position.

---

## Gameplay

Players take turns playing cards from their hand of 5, then buying and moving.

**Card resources:**
- **Skill** — used to buy cards from the card row.
- **Boots** — used to move through dungeon tunnels.
- **Swords** — used to fight monsters.
- **Gold** — spent on dungeon artifacts and market items.

**Clank!** — Many cards generate Clank! (noise). For each Clank! symbol, add one of your colored cubes to the Dragon bag.

**Dragon Attacks:** When certain cards are revealed or artifacts are taken, draw cubes from the bag equal to the Dragon Rage level. Each cube drawn of your color deals 1 damage (HP tracked per player).

**Dungeon movement:** Use Boots to move through tunnels. Deeper rooms have better loot but more danger.

**Artifacts:** Grab artifacts by entering their room. The first artifact taken raises Dragon Rage. High-value artifacts require more Boots to reach.

**Monster combat:** Pay Swords equal to the monster strength to defeat it; take its reward.

---

## Escaping

Exit the dungeon by returning to the start zone. Once a player escapes or dies, the Dragon Rage spikes — remaining players have limited turns to escape or die.

---

## Winning

Total score: artifact value + other treasures + bonus cards - 20 HP damage penalty if you died inside. Escaped players with the most points win. Dead players can still win if their loot was valuable enough.

---

## Tips

- Buying cards that reduce Clank! generation lets you move deeper safely.
- Grabbing a second artifact significantly raises Dragon Rage — plan your exit first.
- Backstab and movement-booster cards are premium purchases early.
