## Overview

Pandemic: On the Brink requires the base Pandemic game to play. It introduces three new scenarios that can be added individually or combined, plus new role cards for more strategic variety.

---

## New Content

**New Roles (6):** Archivist, Containment Specialist, Epidemiologist, Field Operative, Virologist, Contingency Planner. Each has a unique power that changes team strategy.

**5th Disease (Purple):** Adds a purple disease to the board, requiring a 5th cure to win. Some role cards and event cards interact specifically with the purple disease.

---

## Scenarios

### Virulent Strain
Before each game, draw a Virulent Strain card for one of the diseases. This disease behaves differently — it may spread faster, be harder to treat, or trigger additional outbreaks. A separate Virulent Strain deck provides different challenge cards for replayability.

### Mutation
A new Mutation event card is shuffled into the Player deck. When drawn, the purple disease appears and begins spreading. Players must manage a fifth disease simultaneously.

### Bio-Terrorist (Asymmetric)
One player secretly plays as the Bio-Terrorist, adding disease cubes to the board each turn and working against the other players. The other players win by curing all diseases; the Bio-Terrorist wins by causing enough outbreaks or running out disease cubes.

---

## Setup Changes

Add scenario components to the standard Pandemic setup as directed by the chosen scenario card. New role cards are shuffled into the role pool (or selected deliberately).

---

## Winning

Win and loss conditions follow the base Pandemic rules, modified by any active scenario. The Bio-Terrorist scenario adds a separate win condition for the antagonist player.
