## Setup

Place all 16 letter cubes into the covered tray and shake well. Remove the cover to reveal the 4×4 grid. Set the sand timer. All players are ready with pencil and paper.

---

## How to Play

Flip the timer and start hunting for words simultaneously. To form a valid word, trace a path through the grid where each letter cube touches the next horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. **Rules:**

- Each cube may be used only once per word.
- Words must be at least **3 letters** long.
- Proper nouns, abbreviations, and hyphenated words are not allowed.
- The letter "Q" cube always represents "Qu."

Write down every valid word you find. When the timer runs out, all players stop writing.

---

## Scoring

Players read their lists aloud. Any word appearing on **more than one player's list** is crossed off by all players who found it — only unique words score. Valid unique words score:

- 3–4 letters: 1 point
- 5 letters: 2 points
- 6 letters: 3 points
- 7 letters: 5 points
- 8+ letters: 11 points

---

## Winning

The player with the highest total score after an agreed number of rounds wins.

---

## Tips

- Look for prefixes and suffixes to extend short words (RE-, -ING, -ED, -ER).
- Diagonal connections are easy to miss — scan methodically.
- Longer words are worth disproportionately more points; prioritize them over common short words.
