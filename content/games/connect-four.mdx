## Setup

Stand the grid frame upright on a flat surface. Each player takes 21 discs of their color. Decide who goes first.

---

## How to Play

Players alternate turns dropping one disc at a time into any of the seven columns. Discs fall to the lowest available position within the column — you cannot place a disc in mid-air.

A column is **full** once it contains 6 discs; no more discs can be dropped into it.

---

## Winning

The first player to get **four discs in a row** — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins immediately.

**Draw:** If all 42 spaces fill up without either player connecting four, the game is a draw.

---

## Tips

- Control the center column — it participates in more potential four-in-a-row lines than any other.
- Watch for diagonal threats; they are the easiest to miss.
- Look for "double threat" setups where you can win in two different directions simultaneously, forcing your opponent to block only one.
- Block your opponent's three-in-a-rows before building your own offense.
