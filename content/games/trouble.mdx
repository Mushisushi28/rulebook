## Setup

Each player chooses a color and places all 4 pawns in their matching Home area. Players decide who goes first (youngest, or roll to determine).

---

## How to Play

On your turn, press the Pop-O-Matic bubble in the center of the board to roll the die. You must roll a **6** to move a pawn out of Home onto the Start space. Once on the board, pawns move clockwise around the track the number of spaces shown on the die.

**Rolling a 6:** You must move a pawn out of Home or move an existing pawn 6 spaces. You then roll again and take an additional move.

**Sending opponents home:** If you land on a space occupied by an opponent's pawn, that pawn is sent back to its Home area. You cannot land on your own pawns.

**Safe spaces:** Your own Start space is safe — opponents cannot send you home from it.

**Finish line:** To move a pawn into the Finish area (the triangle at the end of the track), you must roll the exact number needed. If you overshoot, you cannot move that pawn.

---

## Winning

The first player to move all 4 pawns into the Finish area wins.

---

## Tips

- Prioritize getting all your pawns out of Home early so you have more options each turn.
- If an opponent's pawn is close to Finish, try to send it back to Home before it gets there.
- Don't bunch your pawns together on the main track — a single opponent roll can chain-send multiple pawns home if not careful.
