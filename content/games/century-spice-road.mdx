## Setup

Lay out 6 Merchant cards face-up in a row. Create a face-down draw pile with remaining Merchant cards. Set out Point cards in a row (5 face-up, rest as draw pile). Each player receives a caravan board, 3 cubes of Turmeric (yellow), and starting hand of 2 Merchant cards (one unique to each starting position).

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, perform exactly one action:

**1. Acquire a Merchant card:** Take the leftmost card in the merchant row for free; pay 1 spice per card you skip past. Slide remaining cards left; refill from draw pile.

**2. Rest:** Take all played cards from your discard pile back into your hand.

**3. Play cards from hand:** Play any number of cards from your hand face-up in front of you. Card effects:
- **Gain** — collect the listed spices (up to caravan capacity of 10 cubes).
- **Trade (upgrade)** — convert listed cubes upward (Yellow → Red → Green → Brown).

**4. Claim a Point card:** Pay the spice cost shown on a face-up Point card and place it in your score pile. Take any gold or silver coins shown on the Point card as bonus points.

Coins: First two players to claim each Point card get a 3-point gold and 1-point silver coin respectively.

---

## Winning

Play until a player has claimed their 5th (2-3 players) or 6th (4-5 players) Point card. Finish the round so everyone has equal turns. Highest total (Point cards + coins) wins. Tiebreaker: most spices remaining.

---

## Tips

- Upgrade cards become exponentially more valuable as the game progresses.
- Claim Point cards early to secure gold coins before competitors.
- Resting efficiently is crucial — time your rest turns around opponent actions.
