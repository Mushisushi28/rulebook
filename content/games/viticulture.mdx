## Setup

Each player receives a player board representing their vineyard, with 3 workers (Grande worker), 1 Rooster, and 1 Harvest token. Deal each player 2 Vine cards, 2 Wine Order cards, and starting resources (coins, bonus card depending on turn order). Set up the seasonal action boards (Spring/Summer/Fall/Winter) and the wine display.

---

## Gameplay

The game is played over years, each divided into 4 seasons.

### Spring
Flip the Wake-Up track card. In turn order (determined by Rooster placement), players choose their wake-up position — higher positions wake earlier but grant smaller bonuses; lower positions wake later but earn better bonuses (bonus card, extra coin, Victory Points, etc.).

### Summer (Growing Season)
Players take turns placing workers on summer action spaces:

- **Draw Vine card** — add vine cards to hand.
- **Play Vine card** — plant a vine (red or white grape type) in your vineyard field.
- **Build structure** — construct buildings (costs coins; unlocks future abilities).
- **Give a tour** — earn 2 coins.
- **Draw Summer Visitor** — special action card for one-time powerful effects.

### Fall
Each player draws one Summer OR Winter Visitor card.

### Winter (Harvest Season)
Players take turns placing workers on winter action spaces:

- **Harvest field** — collect grapes from a planted field (grape value = vine card ratings).
- **Make wine** — convert grape tokens into wine tokens (placed on the Wine Display to age).
- **Fill wine order** — match wine tokens of correct type and value to an Order card. Score VPs and earn residual income.
- **Draw Wine Order card** — add new orders to hand.
- **Train worker** — pay coins to add a worker for future years.
- **Draw Winter Visitor** — special action cards.

**Aging:** At year end, all wine ages up 1 value on the Wine Display.

**Grande worker:** Can be placed on any occupied space, always last.

---

## Winning

First player to reach 20 Victory Points triggers the final year. Finish that year; highest VP wins.

---

## Tips

- Balance vine planting with structure building — Cellars are required to make higher-value wines.
- Visitor cards are extremely powerful; always take one in Fall.
- Filling wine orders with residual tokens provides ongoing income that compounds over years.
