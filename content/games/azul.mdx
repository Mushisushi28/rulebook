## Setup

Place factory displays in the center: 5 for 2 players, 7 for 3, 9 for 4. Fill each with 4 random tiles from the bag. Each player takes a personal board. Place the first-player token in the center.

---

## Gameplay

Each round has two phases.

**Factory Phase:** On your turn, either take all tiles of one color from a factory display (moving leftovers to the center) or take all tiles of one color from the center. The first player to take from the center also takes the first-player token, which goes to your floor line as a penalty.

Place taken tiles onto one of your five pattern rows. All tiles in a row must be the same color, and that color cannot already appear in the corresponding wall row. Overflow goes to your floor line (penalties of -1 to -3 per space).

**Wall-Tiling Phase:** Complete each finished pattern row by moving one tile to the wall; return extras to the lid. Score the placed tile: 1 point if isolated, or count the full horizontal plus full vertical connected line if adjacent to existing tiles. Apply floor penalties and clear the floor.

---

## Game End

The game ends after the round in which any player completes a full horizontal row on their wall. Final bonuses: +2 per completed row, +7 per completed column, +10 for all five of one color.

Most points wins; ties broken by most completed rows.

---

## Tips

- Complete pattern rows before worrying about bonus positioning.
- Blocking opponents from needed colors is often worth sacrificing efficiency.
- The column and color-set bonuses dwarf row bonuses -- plan your wall accordingly.
