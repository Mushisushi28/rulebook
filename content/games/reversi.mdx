## Setup

An 8×8 board. Each player takes 32 discs (one color per side). Place 4 discs in the center in a 2×2 pattern: two diagonally and two in opposite configuration (Black at top-left and bottom-right, White at top-right and bottom-left). Black plays first.

---

## How to Play

On your turn, place one disc on an empty square such that at least one straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) runs from your placed disc to another of your discs, with one or more opponent discs between them. All opponent discs on all such lines are flipped to your color.

**Passing:** If you have no valid move, you must pass. If neither player has a valid move, the game ends immediately.

---

## Winning

When the board is full (or no player can move), count all discs of each color. The player with **more discs** wins.

---

## Tips

- **Corners are permanent** — once captured, they cannot be flipped. Fight for them.
- Avoid placing near corners unless you can take the corner next turn.
- Fewer discs mid-game is often better — fewer discs means fewer opponent moves.
- Edge squares adjacent to corners (X-squares) are the most dangerous squares to occupy.
