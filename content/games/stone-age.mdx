## Setup

Place the main board with resource tracks (food, wood, clay, stone, gold). Each player starts with 5 tribe members, a player board, and 12 food. Place Civilization Cards face-down in rows of 4. Place Building tiles for purchase.

---

## Turn Structure

**Phase 1 — Place Workers:** In turn order, players alternate placing one or more workers on available spaces. Spaces include:

- **Hunting Grounds:** Any number of workers. Roll 1 die per worker; total ÷ 2 = food gained.
- **Forest / Clay Pit / Quarry / River:** Max 7 workers each. Roll per worker; divide by resource-specific number for output (wood ÷ 3, clay ÷ 4, stone ÷ 5, gold ÷ 6).
- **Tool Maker:** 1 worker. Gain a permanent +1 die bonus usable once per round.
- **Hut:** 2 workers (1 man + 1 woman). Add a new tribe member at phase end.
- **Civilization Card row:** 1 worker per card column. Reserve the card.
- **Building tiles:** 1 worker. Commit to purchase.

**Phase 2 — Resolve Workers:** In turn order, collect from each occupied space (roll dice, apply tools, take resources).

**Phase 3 — Feed Tribe:** Pay 1 food per tribe member or take Starvation (-10 VP per missing food).

---

## Winning

Game ends when any Building tile stack is depleted or 4 Civilization Card stacks are gone. Score:
- Building tiles: variable VP.
- Civilization cards: multiplied by matching card sets or collection bonuses.
- Tool count bonuses.
- Tribe size bonuses.

Highest VP wins.

---

## Tips

- Starvation is crippling — always maintain food surplus.
- Civilization card multipliers compound massively — stack matching card types.
- Grow your tribe early to outplace opponents on key resource spots.
