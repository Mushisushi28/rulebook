## Overview

Pandemic Iberia is a standalone Pandemic variant set in 19th-century Spain and Portugal. New mechanics include railway building, water purification, and role-specific historical abilities. Disease names reflect the era: Malaria, Typhus, Yellow Fever, and Cholera.

---

## Setup

Place the Iberian map board. Shuffle 48 Player cards and 48 Infection cards separately. Insert Epidemic cards into the Player deck (based on difficulty). Deal starting infection: flip 9 cards, place disease cubes (3-2-1 on each set). Give each player a Role card and starting hand per the rules.

Place research stations in Lisbon and Madrid.

---

## Core Rules (Same as Pandemic)

Players take 4 actions per turn, then draw 2 Player cards, then infect cities.

**Standard actions available:** Move (drive/ferry), Build Research Station, Treat Disease, Share Knowledge, Discover a Cure (discard 5 matching cards at a research station).

**Outbreak:** If a 4th cube is added to any city, an outbreak occurs — cubes spread to connected cities.

---

## New Mechanics

**Railway building:** Cities can be connected by railways. Building a rail segment costs 2 actions and the appropriate region card. Railways allow faster movement: move along any connected rail network in 1 action.

**Water purification:** The Purify Water action (at an adjacent water source) removes disease cubes from a city without using a Treat action.

**Historical roles:** Each role has abilities tied to the era (e.g., the Nurse treats all disease in a city, the Sailor can move through port cities freely).

---

## Winning

Discover cures for all 4 diseases before running out of Player cards, exhausting disease cubes, or causing 8 outbreaks. Cooperative victory shared by all.
