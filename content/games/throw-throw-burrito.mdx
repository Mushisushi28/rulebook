## Setup

Shuffle all cards and deal 15 to each player. Place the remaining cards in a face-down draw pile in the center. Keep the two foam burritos nearby.

---

## Gameplay

**On your turn:**
1. Draw one card.
2. Pass one card face-down to the player on your left.
3. Play any number of matching sets from your hand face-up in front of you (sets of 3).

The game is simultaneous — everyone draws and passes at the same time as quickly as they want. There is no formal turn order; the speed of play is up to the group.

**Scoring sets:** Collect sets of three matching cards. Each set scores 1 point. Keep sets face-up in front of you.

**Battle cards:** Three special battle cards trigger burrito combat:
- **Burrito War** — All players grab for a burrito. Last two holding burritos throw them at each other until someone is hit. That player takes a Bruise card (-1 point).
- **Burrito Brawl** — The two players with the matching Brawl cards fight a one-on-one burrito duel.
- **Burrito Battle** — One thrower is chosen; all other players are targets.

**Bruise cards:** Bruises subtract from your final score. You cannot discard them.

---

## Winning

When the draw pile runs out, the round ends. Count your completed sets, subtract Bruise cards. Highest score wins.

Play multiple rounds — first to 2 round wins takes the match.
