## Overview

Spot It! (also known as Dobble) is a deck of 55 circular cards. Each card shows 8 symbols drawn from a pool of 50. By mathematical design, any two cards in the deck share exactly one symbol in common. The game includes 5 mini-games using the same deck.

---

## Mini-Game 1: The Tower (2+ players)

Deal one card face-down to each player (starting cards). Place the remainder in a central draw pile face-down. On "go," flip the central pile face-up. All players simultaneously race to find the matching symbol between their personal card and the top card of the central pile. The first player to name the match takes the top card, placing it on their starting card. Continue flipping until all cards are taken. Most cards collected wins.

---

## Mini-Game 2: The Well (3+ players)

Deal all cards face-down to players as starting hands. Place one face-up in the center as the well. Players race to spot the match between any card in their hand and the top well card. First to name it adds that card to the well. First to empty their hand wins.

---

## Mini-Game 3: Hot Potato (3+ players)

Deal cards evenly to all players. Players race to match a card in their hand to the top card of a neighbor's pile, naming the symbol and placing it on them. Try to give away all your cards. Most cards held at the end loses.

---

## Mini-Game 4: Poisoned Gift (3+ players)

Place all cards face-down in the center. Players take turns giving a card to any opponent if they can spot the match with the top card of that opponent's pile. The player who names the match gives the card. Player with most cards at the end loses.

---

## Winning

Each mini-game has its own win condition (most cards, fewest cards, first to empty hand). Pick the variant that suits your group.
