## Setup

Use a 32-card deck (7 through Ace in 4 suits). Deal 10 cards to each player and 2 face-down to the skat (the kitty). Dealer deals in rounds: 3-skat(2)-4-3.

---

## Card Values

Points: Ace=11, Ten=10, King=4, Queen=3, Jack=2, 7-9=0. Total: 120 points.

---

## Bidding

The player to the left of the dealer (Forehand) and the middle player (Middlehand) start bidding. Middlehand says a value; Forehand accepts or passes. The higher bidder then faces the Rearhand (Dealer), same process. The highest bidder becomes Declarer and picks up the skat.

**Game values** are calculated as multiplier x base value. Base values: Clubs=12, Spades=11, Hearts=10, Diamonds=9, Null=23, Null Ouvert=46.

---

## Declaring a Game

After taking the skat, the Declarer discards 2 cards (counted in their final tricks) and announces the game type:

**Suit game:** Name a trump suit. All 4 Jacks are always the highest trumps regardless of named suit (Jack rank: Clubs, Spades, Hearts, Diamonds). The Declarer must win at least 61 card points.

**Grand:** Only the 4 Jacks are trump. Must score 61+.

**Null:** No trump. Declarer must lose every trick. Fail by taking even 1 trick.

---

## Multipliers

The game value is multiplied by:
- **With/Without Jacks:** Count from the Club Jack down -- how many top Jacks are in sequence (whether you hold them or do not). +1 for each.
- **Game itself:** +1 always.
- **Hand:** +1 if skat was not looked at.
- **Schneider:** +1 if winning side scores 90+ (or opponent scores 30 or less).
- **Schwarz:** +1 if winning side takes all tricks.

---

## Winning

Declarer wins if their earned score meets the declared game value AND they met the point threshold. Losing means double the game value is subtracted. Defenders share the points they collect and win by preventing Declarer from reaching 61.

---

## Tips

- Count your sure card points plus expected picks before bidding.
- Null games are high-reward but require holding no stoppers in any suit.
- Schneider and Schwarz bonuses can dramatically swing scores; play aggressively when leading.
