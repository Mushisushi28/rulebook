## Overview

Pandemic Legacy: Season 2 is a standalone cooperative campaign. The world collapsed decades ago; your team maintains Haven cities and delivery networks. Across 12-24 sessions (months), permanent changes are recorded with stickers and pen marks on the board, cards, and character sheets.

---

## Setup (First Session)

Follow the legacy deck instructions to set up the initial map. Place supply cubes, Haven tokens, and character pawns as directed. Each player selects a character role card and takes the starting hand of city cards. Draw the first legacy card to reveal month 1 objectives.

---

## Core Gameplay (Each Scenario)

**Objective:** Complete the month objective (supply cities, connect routes, find relics) before running out of supply cubes or time.

### Player Turns

Each player takes 4 actions per turn from:
- **Move** — travel between connected cities or charter a supply ship.
- **Supply** — place a supply cube on your current city (costs matching city cards from hand).
- **Build/Upgrade** — construct supply stations or upgrade Havens.
- **Share knowledge** — exchange city cards with a co-located player.
- **Special actions** — character-specific abilities.

After your turn:
1. Draw 2 city cards (events may help; population cards may hurt).
2. Reveal and resolve 2 exposure cards — diseases spread, cities lose supplies, or crises emerge.

**Supply depletion:** If any city runs out of supply cubes, a population loss occurs and is recorded permanently. Too many losses end the campaign.

---

## Legacy Elements

- **Stickers** — applied permanently to the board when cities are upgraded, routes open, or events occur.
- **Scars** — permanent negative effects added to characters after traumatic events.
- **Achievements** — unlock new content, characters, or equipment for future months.
- **Retired characters** — removed from play permanently; their legacy lives on the board.

---

## Winning

Complete the month objective to record a victory. Failures are still recorded and the campaign continues — though failures accumulate consequences.

Complete all 12 months to finish the campaign. Final score is based on objectives completed, bonuses earned, and losses avoided.
