## Overview

Dixit Odyssey is a standalone expansion to Dixit. It uses the same dreamlike illustration mechanics with support for larger groups. Core rules are identical to Dixit, with revised voting and scoring for more players.

---

## Setup

Shuffle all 84 illustrated cards. Deal 6 cards to each player. Place the voting track and tokens. The youngest player is the first Storyteller.

---

## Gameplay

### Each Round

1. **Storyteller** chooses one card from their hand and gives a clue — a word, phrase, sound, gesture, or abstract description that relates to the card.
2. **Other players** each secretly choose one card from their own hand that best fits the clue and pass it face-down to the Storyteller.
3. **Shuffle and reveal:** The Storyteller shuffles all submitted cards (theirs included) and lays them face-up numbered 1-N.
4. **Vote:** Each non-Storyteller player secretly votes for the card they believe is the Storyteller card (cannot vote for their own). Reveal votes simultaneously.
5. **Score:**
   - If ALL voters find the Storyteller card OR NONE do: Storyteller scores 0; all other players score 2.
   - Otherwise: Storyteller scores 3; each voter who found the right card scores 3.
   - Non-Storytellers score 1 bonus point per vote their own card received (max 3).
6. All players draw back to 6 cards. Next player clockwise becomes Storyteller.

---

## Winning

First player to reach 30 points wins (or after the deck runs out, highest score wins).

---

## Tips

- Clues that are too obvious or too obscure both score 0 for the Storyteller.
- Target a clue that roughly half the table will understand.
- Choose submitted cards that are thematically ambiguous — you want voters to doubt themselves.
