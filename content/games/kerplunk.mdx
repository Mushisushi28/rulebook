## Setup

Assemble the transparent tube and insert all plastic sticks through the holes to form a web inside the tube. Pour all marbles into the top of the tube so they rest on the stick web. Each player owns one collection tray at the bottom.

---

## Gameplay

Players take turns removing exactly one plastic stick from the tube.

- Any marbles that fall land in the collection trays below.
- Marbles are distributed to the player whose tray they fall into (or split equally if multiple trays catch them).
- The goal is to pull sticks that release as few marbles as possible — ideally none.

**Tension:** As more sticks are removed, the web weakens and each pull risks cascading marble falls.

---

## Winning

After all sticks are removed, each player counts the marbles in their tray. The player with the **fewest** marbles wins. (Unlike most games — fewer is better.)

---

## Variants

**Team play:** Pair up and share trays. Team with fewest marbles combined wins.

**Reverse Kerplunk:** Player with the most marbles wins — strategy shifts to deliberately dropping as many as possible on your turn.
