## Setup

Distribute secret role cards: 1 Sheriff (revealed), 1 Renegade, 2–3 Outlaws, 1–2 Deputies. Deal character cards (unique abilities) to each player and an opening hand. Starting life points = character bullets. Sheriff draws +1 extra card and gets bonus life.

---

## Roles and Goals

- **Sheriff:** Eliminate all Outlaws and the Renegade.
- **Deputies:** Help the Sheriff survive; win if the Sheriff wins.
- **Outlaws:** Kill the Sheriff (they win even if they're dead when the Sheriff dies).
- **Renegade:** Be the last one standing — eliminate everyone including the Sheriff last.

---

## Turn Structure

On your turn:
1. **Draw 2 cards** (or use character special to draw differently).
2. **Play any number of cards** from your hand.
3. **Discard** down to hand limit (= life points remaining).

**Key cards:**
- **BANG!:** Deal 1 damage to any player within range (default range = 1 for adjacent players; weapons extend range).
- **Missed!:** Cancel a BANG! targeting you.
- **Beer:** Regain 1 life (not usable at 1 player remaining).
- **Indians!:** All other players must play a BANG! or lose 1 life.
- **Gatling:** All other players must play a Missed! or lose 1 life.
- **Jail:** Target player skips their turn unless they draw a Heart.

---

## Winning

Win conditions for each role as listed above. When a player is eliminated, discard their cards and reveal their role.

---

## Tips

- Protect the Sheriff even if you're a Deputy; your win is tied to their survival.
- As Renegade, survive by playing both sides until the end — never reveal yourself early.
- The Outlaws win even if they die — sacrifice plays are valid tactics.
