## Overview

Pandemic: Fall of Rome replaces disease cubes with barbarian tokens from five tribes (Gauls, Visigoths, Vandals, Saxons, Huns). Players are Roman leaders defending the empire using legions, alliances, and fortresses. The cooperative goal is to form alliances with all five tribes before Rome falls.

---

## Setup

Place the Roman Empire map. Each player selects a role card and takes legions matching the color indicated. Set barbarian tokens in starting provinces per setup rules. Deal Player cards and insert Invasion cards (equivalent to Epidemics). Place starting fortresses.

---

## Core Rules (Pandemic-Derived)

Players take 4 actions per turn, then draw 2 Player cards, then resolve barbarian invasions.

**Actions:**
- **March** — move legions to adjacent province.
- **Recruit** — gain legions in a province with a Citadel structure.
- **Sack** — remove barbarian tokens by spending matching tribe cards.
- **Fortify** — build a Citadel or Fortress in your province.
- **Form Alliance** — discard 4 matching tribe cards to ally with that tribe. One of five alliance tokens is placed (win condition tracker).
- **Role ability** — use character-specific special action.

**Battles:** Legions and barbarians in the same province fight. Roll combat dice equal to each side count; each sword icon eliminates one opposing unit.

**Invasions:** Like Epidemic cards, Invasion cards surge barbarians into provinces. If a province overflows (more tokens than space), an incursion spreads.

**Rome Overrun:** If barbarians enter Rome itself and cannot be stopped, Rome falls and everyone loses.

---

## Winning

Cooperative: form alliances with all 5 barbarian tribes before running out of Player cards, losing Rome, or triggering 8 incursions.
