## Setup

Each player takes a player board (unique asymmetric layout). Place the central game board with hex tile stacks and silver coin supply. Each player starts with a set of workers (can adjust dice rolls) and initial silver coins. Set up 5 rounds of 5 phases each.

---

## Turn Structure

Each round consists of 5 phases:

**Phase 1:** Reveal goods tiles for the round.
**Phase 2:** Players take turns (2 dice rolls per turn, 2 rounds of turns per phase).
**Phase 3:** Goods delivery scoring (tiles adjacent to specific spaces).
**Phase 4:** Workers check.
**Phase 5:** Silverling income.

**Each turn:** Roll 2 dice. Use each die value for one action:
- **Take a tile** from the corresponding numbered depot space.
- **Place a tile** from your storage to your player board (matching the die value of a target hex).
- **Sell goods** from matching depot numbers for silver.
- **Use a worker** to adjust a die by ±1.

**Tile placement:** Placed tiles must match the hex color and connect to already-placed tiles. Completing a region of the same color scores immediately, with bonuses for earlier completion.

---

## Scoring

- Complete color regions during play (larger = more VP).
- End-game bonuses for most completed region types, most livestock types, mines (silverlings → VP), and passes.

---

## Winning

Most VP at game end wins.

---

## Tips

- Complete regions quickly — earlier completions score significantly more.
- Workers (die adjustment) are the most powerful resource; save them for critical turns.
- Mines provide steady income that funds faster development later.
