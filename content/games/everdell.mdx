## Setup

Set up the Ever Tree centrepiece. Attach the 4 season cards to their places. Shuffle the Meadow cards and deal 8 face-up. Shuffle the remaining deck as the draw pile. Prepare the resource supplies. Each player takes their workers (3 to start) and 2 starting Berries. Deal 5 cards to each player.

---

## Card Types

**Constructions (tan):** Buildings you build in your city. Each construction has a critter pairing -- once built, the matching critter card can be played for free.

**Critters (green/red/blue/purple):** Characters with ongoing or immediate abilities.

**Unique cards (purple):** Powerful cards, only 1 copy exists; worth many end-game points.

---

## Turn Actions

On your turn, take one action:

**Place a worker on a forest location:** Gain listed resources (wood, resin, pebbles, berries) or draw cards. Forest locations are limited -- once occupied, no other worker may use that space.

**Place a worker on a Haven location:** Draw 2 cards, discard any number, gain 1 resource per 2 discarded.

**Place a worker on a basic location:** Gain berries or resources from fixed shared spaces.

**Play a card to your city:** Pay the card resource cost and add it to your city (max 15 cards). Apply any immediate effects.

**Prepare for Season:** When you have no more workers or choose to advance, collect workers back and gain new workers/resources as your season advances (Spring gets 1 new worker + bonus, Summer gets 1 + resource, Autumn gets 2).

---

## Winning

After all players reach Winter and exhaust their actions, score all cards in your city plus any bonus points from special cards. Most points wins.

---

## Tips

- Chain constructions to critters: a Carpenter Construction lets you play the Husband critter free.
- Purple unique cards are worth 4+ points and powerful effects -- prioritize them when available.
- Managing your city size (15 card limit) is critical; plan what you want to include from turn 3 onward.
