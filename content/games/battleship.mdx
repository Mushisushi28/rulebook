## Setup

Each player takes an ocean grid unit with two grids: the lower grid for your fleet and the upper grid to track your shots. Place your ships secretly on your lower grid — ships may be placed horizontally or vertically but not diagonally, and they may not overlap or extend off the grid.

**Standard fleet:**
- Carrier — 5 spaces
- Battleship — 4 spaces
- Destroyer — 3 spaces
- Submarine — 3 spaces
- Patrol Boat — 2 spaces

---

## How to Play

Players alternate turns calling out a coordinate (a letter and a number, e.g., "B-7"). Your opponent checks their lower grid and responds:

- **"Hit"** if one of their ships occupies that coordinate.
- **"Miss"** if the coordinate is open water.

Mark your upper tracking grid with a **red peg** for a hit and a **white peg** for a miss. Your opponent marks their lower fleet grid to track the hits on their ships.

**Sinking a ship:** When all coordinates of a ship have been hit, the owner must announce which ship was sunk: "You sank my Battleship!"

---

## Winning

The first player to sink all five of their opponent's ships wins the game.

---

## Tips

- After a hit, methodically check adjacent squares (up, down, left, right) to find the ship's orientation.
- Once you determine a ship's direction, continue along that axis until the ship sinks.
- Space your ships apart to make them harder to find by lucky adjacent guesses.
