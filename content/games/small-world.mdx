## Setup

Choose the correct side of the board based on player count. Set out the visible Race/Special Power combo banners. Each player starts with a fixed number of gold coins. Shuffle the Race banners and Special Power tiles separately; combine them into random Race-Power combos along the board edge (the queue).

---

## Choosing a Race

The first race combo is free. Lower combos cost 1 coin per position from the top and those coins stay on the banner (bonus for whoever takes that combo later). Take the race banner and all its tokens plus the special power tile; read both to understand your combo abilities.

---

## Conquest

On your turn, conquer adjacent territories in any order. Each territory costs a base of 2 tokens to conquer plus 1 per enemy token already there. Some terrains cost +1. Roll the Reinforcement die (0–3) for your final conquest of the turn if you fall short by 1–3 tokens.

After conquering, spread remaining tokens however you want across your regions.

---

## In Decline

When your race is overextended, put it "In Decline": flip all tokens face-down, score 1 coin per region still held at round end. Your In Decline race stays until eliminated. On the next turn, choose a new Race/Power combo and begin again.

---

## Scoring

Each round, score 1 coin per region you control with your active race plus any In Decline coins.

---

## Winning

After a set number of rounds, count coins. Most coins wins.

---

## Tips

- Going into decline at the right moment is as important as conquering aggressively.
- Race/Power combos have massive synergy variance — read them together.
- Target opponents' In Decline tokens; they're easy to dislodge and worth preventing VP.
