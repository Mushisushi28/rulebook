## Setup

Divide into two teams. Choose one player from the first team to be the Clue-giver. That player draws a card from the deck. Each card shows one **Guess Word** (big word at top) and five **Taboo words** (the forbidden clues). Set the 60-second timer.

---

## How to Play

The Clue-giver tries to get their teammates to say the Guess Word by giving verbal clues only. They may not:
- Say the Guess Word or any form of it.
- Say any of the 5 Taboo words or forms of them.
- Give hand gestures or sounds.

A member of the opposing team watches the Clue-giver's card (the "squeaker" holds the buzzer). If the Clue-giver says a Taboo word, skips a card, or breaks a rule, the squeaker buzzes and that card is discarded without scoring.

When the timer runs out, the next team takes their turn.

---

## Scoring

- **+1 point** for each card the team correctly guesses.
- **-1 point** (or card returned to bottom of deck) for each card that was buzzed or skipped.

---

## Winning

After an agreed number of rounds (or when the card deck runs out), the team with the most points wins.

---

## Tips

- Use synonyms, scenarios ("it's what you do when..."), or examples rather than definitions.
- Practice using the forbidden words to see how central they are, then work around them.
- Fast teams can score 6–8 cards per minute; aim for quantity over perfection.
