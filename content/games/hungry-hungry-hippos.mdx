## Setup

Assemble the game board with four hippo units clipped to the corners. Each player operates one hippo. Place all marbles into the center trough. Do not release them yet.

---

## Gameplay

On a countdown (or signal), release all marbles into the play area simultaneously. Each player rapidly presses the tail lever on their hippo, causing the hippo head to chomp outward and scoop marbles back toward their collection zone.

- **Chomping:** Press the lever repeatedly as fast as possible. Speed is the only skill.
- **No turns:** All players play simultaneously until all marbles are claimed.
- **Dropped marbles:** Any marble that falls off the board is out of play.

---

## Winning

When all marbles have been eaten or have fallen off, each player counts the marbles in their hippo's collection area. The player with the most marbles wins.

**Variant — Lucky Marble:** Designate one white or special marble as the Lucky Marble. If you capture it, you win the round regardless of total count.
