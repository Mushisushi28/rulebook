## Setup

Select role cards based on player count (see the included table for good vs. evil ratios). Good roles include Loyal Servant of Arthur, Merlin, Percival, and others. Evil roles include Minion of Mordred, Assassin, Morgana, and Oberon.

Shuffle and deal one role card face-down to each player. Players look at their card secretly.

**Sight phase (eyes closed):** Evil players silently identify each other with eyes open. Then Merlin opens their eyes and sees all evil players (except Oberon, if in play). Percival sees Merlin and Morgana (if in play) but cannot tell who is who.

---

## Gameplay

The game is played over up to 5 Quest missions.

**Team Selection:** The Leader proposes a team of the required size (shown on the mission board). All players vote by flipping Approve/Reject tokens simultaneously. If a majority approves, the team goes on the Quest. If rejected, leadership passes left and a new team is proposed. After 5 rejected teams in a row, evil wins automatically.

**Quest Phase:** Each player on the approved team secretly plays either a Success or Fail card. Good players must play Success; evil players may play either. Reveal all cards.

- 1+ Fail cards: Mission Fails (red token).
- All Success: Mission Succeeds (blue token).

**Special rule:** Some missions at higher player counts require 2 Fail cards to fail the mission.

---

## Winning

Good wins if 3 Quests succeed. Evil wins if 3 Quests fail.

**Assassination Phase:** If Good wins 3 Quests, the Assassin gets one final chance to name who they think Merlin is. If they identify Merlin correctly, Evil wins instead.
