## Setup

Shuffle the deck of 64 cards. Deal cards face-down evenly to all players. Players hold their stack face-down without looking at it.

---

## Gameplay

Players take turns flipping the top card from their stack onto a central pile, face-up. As each card is flipped, the active player says the next word in sequence: Taco, Cat, Goat, Cheese, Pizza, then back to Taco. The sequence repeats continuously.

**When the spoken word matches the card just flipped**, all players race to slap the central pile. The LAST player to slap takes all the cards in the pile and adds them to the bottom of their stack.

**Special cards:**
- **Gorilla:** All players pound their chest twice, then slap.
- **Narwhal:** All players point their hands like a horn above their head, then slap.
- **Groundhog:** All players pound the table twice with both fists, then slap.

For special cards, the last player to complete the action AND slap takes the pile.

---

## Winning

The first player to get rid of all their cards wins. If you flip all your cards away and the game is not over yet, you are eliminated from the active rotation but may continue to slap until the end.

---

## Tips

- The hardest moment is when the word you say matches the card, because you must simultaneously say it AND slap — some players instinctively hesitate.
- False slaps (slapping when there is no match) also penalize you: you must give one card to every other player.
