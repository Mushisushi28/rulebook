## Setup

Shuffle the Island tiles and lay them in a diamond pattern (see rulebook for the exact shape). Shuffle the Flood deck and flip tiles to flood initial spaces (one per player + 2 extra). Deal each player a role card with a unique ability. Shuffle the Treasure deck and deal 2 cards to each player. Place treasures at their designated island tiles.

---

## Roles

**Pilot:** May fly to any island tile once per turn.
**Engineer:** May shore up 2 tiles for 1 action.
**Explorer:** May move and shore up diagonally.
**Diver:** May move through flooded and missing tiles.
**Messenger:** May give Treasure cards to any player regardless of location.
**Navigator:** May move other players up to 2 spaces for 2 actions.

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, take up to 3 actions (in any combination):
- Move to an adjacent tile (up, down, left, right)
- Shore up a flooded adjacent tile (flip it back to dry side)
- Give a Treasure card to another player on your tile
- Capture a treasure (if on the matching tile with 4 cards of that treasure type -- discard all 4)

After actions, draw 2 Treasure deck cards. If you draw a Waters Rise card, move it out of play, shuffle the used Flood deck cards, and place them on top. Increase the Flood Meter by 1.

Then flip Flood deck cards equal to the current Flood Level and flip those island tiles to their flooded (blue) side. A flooded tile flipped again sinks -- remove it from the game permanently.

---

## Winning

Collect all 4 treasures, get all players to Fool Landing tile, and use a Helicopter Lift card to escape. If any treasure site sinks before being claimed, or Fool Landing sinks, or a player is stranded on a sinking tile, or the Flood Meter reaches the skull, everyone loses.

---

## Tips

- Prioritize shoring up the treasure sites and Fool Landing above all else.
- Share cards freely -- you need exactly 4 of a kind to capture a treasure.
- The Navigator and Messenger are especially powerful at keeping teammates positioned.
