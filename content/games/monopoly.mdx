## Setup

Place the board. Each player chooses a token and places it on GO. Each player receives $1,500 in starting money: 2×$500, 2×$100, 2×$50, 6×$20, 5×$10, 5×$5, and 5×$1. Shuffle the Chance and Community Chest decks and place them face-down on their board spaces. The banker manages all remaining money, Title Deed cards, houses, and hotels.

---

## Gameplay

Players take turns rolling two dice and moving their token clockwise the number of spaces shown.

- **Landing on an unowned property:** You may buy it at the printed price, or the bank auctions it to the highest bidder.
- **Landing on an owned property:** Pay rent to the owner. Rent increases when the owner holds all properties in a color group.
- **Landing on GO:** Collect $200.
- **Landing on a Tax space:** Pay the printed amount to the bank.
- **Landing on Chance/Community Chest:** Draw a card and follow its instructions.
- **Going to Jail:** You may be sent to Jail by a card, landing on "Go to Jail," or rolling doubles three times in a row. While in Jail you cannot collect rent on unmortgaged properties unless you use a Get Out of Jail Free card, pay a $50 fine, or roll doubles within three turns.

**Doubles:** Rolling doubles earns an extra turn, but rolling doubles three consecutive times sends you to Jail.

**Building:** Once you own all properties of a color group, you may build houses (and then hotels) on those properties. Houses must be built evenly across the group — you cannot put a second house on any property until all have one house.

**Mortgaging:** You may mortgage a property to the bank for half its printed price. No rent can be collected on mortgaged properties. Pay 110% of the mortgage value to unmortgage.

---

## Winning

The last player who has not gone bankrupt wins. A player goes bankrupt when they cannot pay what they owe to another player or the bank and have no assets to raise the funds.
