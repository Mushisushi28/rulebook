## Setup

Place the board. Each player chooses a token and receives $1,500 in starting money (2×$500, 4×$100, 1×$50, 1×$20, 2×$10, 1×$5, 5×$1). Shuffle the Chance and Community Chest decks. Youngest player goes first.
