## Setup

Shuffle the 108-card deck. Deal **7 cards** to each player. Place the remaining deck face-down as the Draw Pile. Flip the top card to start the Discard Pile. If the first card is a Wild or Wild Draw Four, return it to the middle and flip again. If it is an action card, its effect applies immediately.

Youngest player goes first. Play proceeds clockwise.

---

## Card Types

**Numbered cards (0–9):** Four colors — Red, Yellow, Green, Blue. 19 cards per color (one 0, two each of 1–9).

**Action cards (76 of them per full deck):**
- **Skip** — Next player loses their turn.
- **Reverse** — Reverses direction of play (in 2-player, acts as Skip).
- **Draw Two** — Next player draws 2 cards and loses their turn.
- **Wild** — Player declares any color; play continues in that color.
- **Wild Draw Four** — Player declares color AND next player draws 4 cards and loses their turn. **May only be played if you have no cards matching the current color** (honor system).

---

## Turn Structure

On your turn, play exactly one card by placing it on the Discard Pile. You may play a card if it matches the top Discard card by:
- **Color** (any number or action of the same color)
- **Number** (any color with the same number)
- **Type** (e.g., Skip on Skip, Draw Two on Draw Two, regardless of color)
- It is a **Wild** or **Wild Draw Four** (always playable, Wild Draw Four with the restriction above)

**If you cannot play:** Draw 1 card from the Draw Pile. If that drawn card is playable, you **may** play it immediately or keep it. Either way, your turn ends.

**Empty Draw Pile:** Shuffle the Discard Pile (except the top card) to form a new Draw Pile.

---

## Calling "UNO!"

When you play your **second-to-last card**, leaving only 1 card in your hand, you **must** shout **"UNO!"** before the next player takes their turn.

If you forget and another player catches you (before the next player starts their turn), you must draw **4 penalty cards**. You cannot be penalized after the next player begins their turn.

---

## Stacking (House Rule — Not Official)

The official rules do not permit stacking Draw Two or Draw Four cards. Each Draw card affects only the immediate next player, who must draw and lose their turn.

---

## Challenging Wild Draw Four

A player who is forced to draw 4 may **challenge** the player who played the Wild Draw Four. The challenged player must show their hand. If they had a matching color card (making the Wild Draw Four illegal), they must draw 4 instead of the challenger. If the Wild Draw Four was legal, the challenger draws 6 cards instead of 4.

---

## Winning

The first player to play all cards from their hand wins the round. In multi-round play, score points based on cards remaining in opponents' hands:
- Numbered cards: face value
- Skip, Reverse, Draw Two: 20 pts each
- Wild, Wild Draw Four: 50 pts each

First player to accumulate **500 points** wins the game.
