## Setup

Place Cavity Sam (the patient board) in the center. Insert all ailment pieces into their corresponding slots on the board. Shuffle the Specialist cards (one per ailment) and place them in a draw pile. Each player starts with some play money (or use a simple winner-takes-most scoring method).

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, draw a Specialist card. It shows which ailment you must remove and the cash reward for success. Use the metal tweezers to extract the matching piece from the patient without touching the metal edges of the cavity.

If you successfully remove the piece without buzzing, collect the cash reward shown on the card and return the piece to the board for future players.

If the buzzer sounds (you touched the metal edge), you earn nothing and your turn ends. The piece stays in the board.

Play passes clockwise. When all Specialist cards are exhausted, tally up each player earnings.

---

## Winning

The player who has earned the most money when all cards are used wins.

---

## Variants

**Casual play:** Skip the money; players simply try to collect the most pieces. First to reach a target count wins.

**Cooperative:** All players work together to remove all pieces before a sand timer runs out.

---

## Tips

- Hold the tweezers lightly -- a death grip makes your hands shake.
- Go for the largest, easiest-to-reach pieces first to build confidence (and cash).
- Breathing steadily and moving slowly prevents the small muscle tremors that cause buzzes.
