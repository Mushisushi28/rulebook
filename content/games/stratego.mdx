## Setup

Each player secretly arranges their 40 pieces on the 4 rows nearest them. Pieces face away from the opponent. Two 2×2 lake areas in the center are impassable.

**Piece ranks (highest to lowest):** Marshal (10), General (9), Colonel (8), Major (7), Captain (6), Lieutenant (5), Sergeant (4), Miner (3), Scout (2), Spy (1), Bomb (B), Flag (F).

---

## How to Play

Players alternate moving one piece one square horizontally or vertically (no diagonal). Bombs and the Flag cannot move. **Scouts** may move any number of squares in a straight line.

**Attacking:** Move onto an opponent's piece to reveal both pieces. The higher-ranked piece wins and stays; the lower-ranked piece is removed. Equal ranks: both are removed.

**Special rules:**
- **Bomb:** Defeats any piece that attacks it, except Miners.
- **Miner (3):** Defeats Bombs when attacking them.
- **Spy (1):** Defeats the Marshal (10) when the Spy attacks — but the Spy loses to everything else.

---

## Winning

Capture the opponent's **Flag** to win. If all of a player's moveable pieces are eliminated (unable to move), they also lose.

---

## Tips

- Use expendable low-rank pieces to probe for the Flag and Bombs.
- Keep your Marshal and General protected in early game.
- Cluster Bombs around your Flag but leave Miners accessible for late-game bomb-clearing.
- Bluff with piece positioning to mislead your opponent.
