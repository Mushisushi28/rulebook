## Setup

Set up the Colony board and surrounding location boards. Place zombies and item cards as indicated by the selected Scenario card (which sets the colony objective and rounds). Each player receives 2 survivor cards and a Secret Objective card — objectives must be kept hidden. One player may secretly be the Betrayer with a conflicting objective. Set the Morale and Food tracks.

---

## Gameplay

Each round represents one day of the crisis. Players take turns before a colony phase resolves.

### Player Turn

You have a number of dice equal to your survivors in play. Spend dice to:

- **Move a survivor** to an adjacent location (costs 1 die; moving through zombies triggers exposure).
- **Search** a location (die value determines success; find items from the location deck).
- **Attack zombies** (die value must meet the kill threshold; noisy attacks draw more zombies).
- **Special action** (use an item card, play an event, or perform a survivor ability).

**Exposure rolls:** Any time a survivor moves through or stays with zombies unprotected, roll the exposure die. Results include: nothing, frostbite (a wound counter), wound (remove a survivor card), or kill (eliminate the survivor entirely).

### Colony Phase (End of Round)

1. **Add zombies** to locations based on zombie cards drawn.
2. **Spend food** from the colony supply (-1 per survivor in play). If food is empty, lose 1 Morale.
3. **Resolve the crisis** — players collectively contributed items matching the crisis demand (or not).
4. **Check win/loss:** If Morale hits 0, everyone loses. If the Scenario objective is complete and all survivors have met their secret objectives, they win together.

---

## Secret Objectives and Betrayal

Some scenarios include a Betrayer role dealt from a separate deck. The Betrayer wins only if the colony fails. They may subtly sabotage food, attract zombies, or withhold contributions — but must avoid being exiled.

**Exile vote:** At any time between turns, a player may call an exile vote against any other player. Majority vote exiles that player (and their survivors). If the Betrayer is exiled, good survivors win automatically. If an innocent is exiled, the Betrayer gets a stronger win condition.

---

## Winning

Complete the Scenario objective before Morale reaches 0. All surviving players who also fulfilled their personal secret objective win. Betrayer wins if the colony collapses.
