## Setup

Lay out the 10x10 kingdom grid. Shuffle the 181 cards into the draft deck. Deal 10 cards to each player for the first draft. Each player places one Bunny meeple on a random starting territory.

---

## Gameplay

The game is played over 4 rounds of drafting.

### Draft Phase

Each player simultaneously picks one card and passes their hand to the left. Players draft until each has kept a set number of cards (varies by player count). Cards come in three types:

- **Territory cards** — claim a square on the grid by placing a Bunny there.
- **Building cards** — place a City or Tower on a territory you already own.
- **Parchment cards** — kept secret; score bonus points at game end for meeting conditions.

### Build Phase

After drafting, play all drafted cards:
1. Place Bunnies on claimed territories.
2. Place Cities and Towers on your territories.

---

## Scoring

**Fiefdoms:** At game end, identify your connected groups of territories. Each fiefdom scores: (number of Bunnies) x (number of Cities in the fiefdom) x (total resource value of the fiefdom). Resources come from terrain types on each square.

**Parchment cards:** Score bonuses for meeting their unique conditions (most cities, most of a resource, etc.).

---

## Tips

- Draft territory cards that expand existing fiefdoms rather than starting new isolated groups.
- Towers double the value of their territory.
- Some Parchment cards are extremely powerful — prioritize recognizing and blocking opponent Parchments.
