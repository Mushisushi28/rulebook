## Setup

Shuffle the 24 City tiles face-down and lay them in a 5x5 grid with a gap in the center (Storm Eye space). Deal each player a role card. Place Sand tiles on several starting locations per the rulebook. Set the Storm Meter to Moderate. Give each player their starting water supply. Shuffle the Storm deck.

---

## Roles

**Archaeologist:** Can remove 2 sand from a tile or adjacent tile per action.
**Climber:** Can move through sand-blocked tiles and carry another player.
**Explorer:** Can move and remove sand diagonally.
**Meteorologist:** Can look at upcoming Storm cards; may reduce water loss from the sun.
**Navigator:** Can move other players up to 3 spaces.
**Water Carrier:** Can pick up extra water from oasis tiles; carries extra capacity.

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, take up to 4 actions: move, excavate a tile, pick up a flying machine part (if found), remove sand, or give/take water from a player on the same tile.

After actions, draw Storm cards equal to the current Storm Level. Each card moves the Storm Eye in a direction, burying tiles under sand markers. If a tile accumulates 2+ sand markers, it is impassable. After movement, the Storm Level increases.

Tiles must be excavated (flipped) to reveal what is underneath. Part locations are cross-referenced across two tiles -- the row tile and column tile of each part must both be found and excavated to locate where the part is buried.

---

## Winning

Find all 4 flying machine parts, get all players to the excavated launch pad, and fly away before any player runs out of water or the sand marker supply is exhausted (sandstorm wins).

---

## Tips

- Water management is critical. Share water before players are dangerously low.
- Excavate tiles systematically to locate parts faster.
- Keep the Storm Eye away from the launch pad.
