## Setup

Give each player an answer pad and a folder. Place a category list card (1 of 250+) in each folder. Roll the 20-sided letter die to determine the round's letter. Set the 3-minute timer. All players must use the same letter for all 12 categories on the card.

---

## How to Play

When the timer starts, players simultaneously write one answer for each of the 12 categories that begins with the rolled letter. Alliteration bonus: answers consisting of two or more words all starting with the letter score double (if challenged and upheld).

When time expires, players take turns reading their answers aloud.

**Scoring:**
- Any answer that **no other player** has (or an equivalent answer) scores 1 point (or 2 for valid alliteration).
- Duplicate answers — answers matching another player's response — score 0 for all who wrote it.
- Players may challenge answers for validity; the group votes.

---

## Winning

After three rounds (each with a different letter), tally all points. The player with the highest total wins.

---

## Tips

- Think of unusual answers — common words that everyone will write score nothing.
- For tough categories, settle for any valid answer rather than leaving a blank.
- Alliteration doubles your points but is easy to challenge — only use it if the answer is genuinely valid.
