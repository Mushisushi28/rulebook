## Setup

Standard 52-card deck, 13 cards dealt to each player. Before play, each player passes 3 cards face-down to another player (direction rotates each hand: left, right, across, and keep).

---

## How to Play

The player holding the 2 of Clubs leads first. Players must follow the lead suit if able; if not, they may play any card. The **highest card of the lead suit** wins each trick; the winner leads next.

**Penalty cards:**
- Each Heart = 1 point
- Queen of Spades = 13 points

**Bleeding:** Hearts cannot be led until hearts are "broken" (a heart or the Queen of Spades has been discarded) — unless the leader has only hearts.

The 2 of Clubs must be led first, and the first trick cannot contain penalty cards (no hearts, no Queen of Spades).

---

## Shooting the Moon

If one player takes **all 13 Hearts and the Queen of Spades** in a single hand, they "Shoot the Moon." Instead of receiving 26 points, all other players receive 26 points (or the shooter reduces their own score by 26, player's choice).

---

## Winning

Play continues until any player reaches **100 points**. At that point, the player with the **lowest score** wins.

---

## Tips

- Pass your highest cards in the passing phase to dump points elsewhere.
- The Queen of Spades is the most dangerous card — pass her if you can't control the suit.
- If you're trying to Shoot the Moon, protect your high hearts and aim to void other suits quickly.
