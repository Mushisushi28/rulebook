## Setup

Each player takes a mouse token and places it at the start space. Shuffle the cheese wedge pile. No trap pieces are assembled at the start — the trap is built during play.

---

## Gameplay

Players take turns rolling the die and moving their mouse clockwise around the board.

**Board spaces:**
- **Build** — Add one trap piece to the trap structure as shown on the card drawn.
- **Safe** — Nothing happens.
- **Cheese** — Take a cheese wedge.
- **Lose Cheese** — Return a cheese wedge to the pile.
- **Turn Crank** — If any opponent is on the Cheese Wheel space and the trap is complete, turn the crank and attempt to catch them.

**Building the trap:** Players add pieces in order as they land on Build spaces. Once all pieces are placed, the trap is operational.

**Springing the trap:** When you land on the Turn Crank space (or trigger it by passing), turn the crank. The steel ball rolls down the chute, knocking the diving man into the tub, which tips the cage onto the Cheese Wheel space. Any mouse caught there is eliminated.

---

## Winning

Last mouse remaining on the board wins. Alternatively, the first player to collect 6 cheese wedges wins (beginner variant).
