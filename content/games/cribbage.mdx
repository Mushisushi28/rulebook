## Setup

Standard 52-card deck. Use a cribbage board with two tracks (60 or 121 holes) and 2 pegs per player to track score with leap-frog pegging. Non-dealer deals 6 cards to each player (2-player). Each player chooses 2 cards to discard face-down into the **Crib** — an extra hand scored by the dealer after play.

Cut the remaining deck; the top card is the **starter** (if it's a Jack, dealer pegs 2 — "His heels" or "Nibs").

---

## Play Phase

Non-dealer leads a card face-up, stating its value. Players alternate playing cards and announcing the running total. Card values: Ace = 1, 2–10 = face value, J/Q/K = 10. Running total may not exceed **31**.

**Scoring during play:**
- **Fifteen:** Running total hits exactly 15 = 2 points.
- **Thirty-one:** Running total hits 31 = 2 points; reset to 0.
- **Pair:** Playing a card matching the previous rank = 2 points.
- **Pair Royal:** Third same rank = 6 points.
- **Double Pair Royal:** Fourth same rank = 12 points.
- **Run:** Three or more cards in sequence (any order) = 1 point per card.
- **Last Card:** Playing the final card without reaching 31 = 1 point ("Go").

---

## Counting the Hand

After play, count your hand (plus the starter) for points:
- **Fifteen:** Any combination summing to 15 = 2 points each.
- **Pairs:** 2 points per pair.
- **Runs:** 1 point per card in any run of 3+.
- **Flush:** 4 cards same suit in hand = 4 points (5 including starter = 5).
- **Nobs:** Jack in hand matching starter suit = 1 point.

Non-dealer counts first. Dealer counts hand, then crib.

---

## Winning

First player to **121 points** wins — you may "peg out" during play to win immediately without counting your hand.

---

## Tips

- Keep high-scoring cards (5s are especially powerful — they pair with 10-value cards for 15s).
- Discard low-value connectors to the opponent's crib; discard high-scoring pairs to your own crib when dealer.
