## Setup

Shuffle the caption cards and deal 7 to each player. Place the photo cards face-down in a pile. Choose a starting judge; the role rotates clockwise each round.

---

## Gameplay

### Each Round

1. **Reveal:** The judge flips the top photo card and places it where everyone can see.
2. **Caption:** All non-judges choose one caption card from their hand and submit it face-down to the judge.
3. **Judge:** The judge shuffles the submitted captions and reads each aloud while holding the photo. The judge picks their favorite.
4. **Winner:** The player who submitted the winning caption keeps the photo card as a point. All players draw back up to 7 caption cards.
5. **Rotate:** The next player clockwise becomes the new judge.

**No debating the judge** — their decision is final.

---

## Winning

First player to collect a set number of photo cards wins:
- 3-5 players: 7 cards
- 6-10 players: 5 cards
- 11+ players: 4 cards

---

## Tips

- Know your audience — tailor captions to the current judge.
- Absurdist humor often beats obvious puns.
- Cards that reference inside jokes with your group consistently win.
