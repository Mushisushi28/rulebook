## Setup

Each player draws 6 tiles from the bag without showing them. Remaining tiles form the draw pile. The oldest player goes first.

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, place one or more tiles from your hand onto the table, then draw replacements to refill your hand to 6.

**Placement rules:**
- All tiles played on a single turn must share one attribute (all same color OR all same shape).
- Every tile placed must connect to at least one existing tile.
- Each line formed must contain tiles that are all the same color OR all the same shape — never both and never duplicates within a line.
- A line can contain at most 6 tiles (one of each shape or color).

**Scoring:**
- Count every tile in every line your new tiles complete or extend — including the tiles already on the board in those lines.
- A **Qwirkle** — completing a line of 6 — scores 6 bonus points (12 total for that line).

**Cannot play:** If you have no legal play, discard any number of tiles face-down and draw the same number.

---

## Winning

When the draw bag is empty and a player uses their last tile, the game ends. That player earns a 6-point bonus. The player with the most points wins.

---

## Tips

- Build toward Qwirkles rather than short-term points.
- Block opponents from completing lines by filling in strategic positions.
- Pay attention to which shapes and colors are already in each line before playing.
