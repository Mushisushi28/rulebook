## Setup

Place the two-sided island board (use the basic side for first game). Set up the research track, artifact row, item row, and guardian row. Each player receives an identical starter deck of 6 cards and 2 starting tokens. Place all workers (2 per player) beside the board. Set the round marker to Round 1.

---

## Gameplay

The game lasts 5 rounds. On your turn, play cards from your hand and take one action:

**Actions:**
- **Dig at a site** — place a worker on a newly discovered or known expedition site. Gain the listed resources and cards.
- **Discover a new site** — move to an undiscovered island location, paying the travel cost. Flip the site tile to reveal resources and a Guardian card. Defeat or ignore the Guardian.
- **Research** — spend Compasses to advance your token on the Research track. Gain the listed bonus for each space passed and the space you stop on.
- **Buy a card** — purchase an Item or Artifact from the row by spending coin/resources. Add it to your discard pile.
- **Play a card** — some cards grant free actions; others generate resources or chain effects.
- **Overcome a Guardian** — pay the Fear and resource cost to defeat a Guardian at a site you occupy. Gain the reward.
- **Pass** — end your turn.

**Resources:** Compasses (research/exploration), Tablets (research bonuses), Arrowheads (Guardian combat), Jewels (card purchases), Fear cards (clutter your deck if you flee Guardians).

**Deck flow:** Standard deck-builder — play hand, discard, redraw. Your deck grows as you purchase new cards.

---

## End of Round

After all players pass, advance the round marker. Draw 5 new cards.

---

## Winning

After 5 rounds, score:
- Artifacts owned.
- Research track position and bonuses.
- Sites discovered.
- Cards with point values.
- Idol tokens collected.
- Subtract 1 VP per Fear card remaining in deck/discard.

Highest score wins.

---

## Tips

- Research track bonuses are powerful and scale late — invest early.
- Fear cards punish players who flee Guardians; defeat them for double benefit.
- Artifacts are expensive but provide unique abilities unavailable on the item row.
