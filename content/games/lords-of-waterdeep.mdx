## Setup

Place the Waterdeep city board. Each player takes 2 Agent tokens (3 for 2 players), an anonymous Lord of Waterdeep card, and a player board. Deal 2 Quest cards and 2 Intrigue cards to each player. Place resource cubes (Fighters, Rogues, Wizards, Clerics) in the supply. First player gets the Ambassador.

---

## Round Structure (8 rounds)

**Assign Agents:** In turn order, each player places one agent on an unoccupied action space and collects the listed resources or effects. Repeat until all agents are assigned.

**Action spaces include:**
- **Castle Waterdeep:** Take the First Player token; draw an Intrigue card.
- **Cliffwatch Inn:** Take face-up Quest cards; option to take Intrigue cards.
- **Buildings:** Gain resources, complete quests, or trigger special abilities.
- **Builder's Hall:** Purchase a new building to place on the board (scoring VP and providing income to the owner when used by others).

**Complete Quests:** Spend required adventurer cubes (Fighters, Rogues, Wizards, Clerics) and gold to complete a Quest card and score VP plus rewards.

**Play Intrigue cards:** Most spaces allow or require playing Intrigue cards (grant resources, hinder opponents, or extend quest completions).

**Recall Agents:** At end of round, all agents return to their owners.

---

## Winning

After 8 rounds, score remaining completed quest VP, building VP, Lord bonuses (secret multipliers for specific quest types), and leftover resources (1 VP per 2 gold). Highest score wins.

---

## Tips

- Prioritize quests that match your Lord's category for maximum bonus VP.
- Placing buildings creates a passive income stream when opponents use them.
- Mandatory Quest Intrigue cards can strangle opponents' plans — use them early.
