## Setup

Shuffle all **72 land tiles** face-down. Flip the **starting tile** (marked with a dark back) and place it in the center. Each player takes **7 meeples** of their color and places 1 on the scoring track at 0.

The player who most recently visited France goes first (or youngest).

---

## Turn Structure

On every turn, do these three steps:

### 1. Draw and Place a Tile

Draw 1 tile from the shuffled pile and place it face-up adjacent to an existing tile. The tile must be placed so all edges that touch another tile's edges continue the same features — grass to grass, road to road, city to city.

If a tile cannot be legally placed anywhere, discard it and draw another.

### 2. Deploy a Meeple (Optional)

After placing a tile, you **may** place 1 meeple from your supply onto the tile you just placed. Place it on one of the features on that tile:

- **City segment** → your meeple becomes a **Knight**
- **Road segment** → your meeple becomes a **Highwayman**
- **Farm field** → your meeple becomes a **Farmer**
- **Monastery** → your meeple becomes a **Monk**

**Restriction:** You cannot place a meeple on a feature that is already occupied by another meeple (even your own), even if that feature spans multiple tiles and the meeple is on a different tile.

### 3. Score Completed Features

A feature is **completed** when it is fully enclosed:
- A **city** is complete when all its edges are surrounded by city tiles (fully enclosed).
- A **road** is complete when both ends are capped (by a city, another road junction, or the road loops back to itself).
- A **monastery** is complete when surrounded on all 8 sides by tiles.

**When a feature completes:**
- The player(s) with the most meeples on that feature score it.
- All tied players score full points.
- Return all meeples from that feature to their owners.

**Scoring for completed features:**
| Feature | Points |
|---|---|
| City | 2 pts per tile, +2 per pennant shield |
| Road | 1 pt per tile |
| Monastery | 9 pts (1 for itself + 1 per surrounding tile) |

---

## Farms (Fields)

Farmers are **never returned** during the game. They score only at game end. Farms feed completed cities — at game end, each farm region scores **3 points per completed city** it is adjacent to, for the majority farmer.

---

## Shared Majorities

If two or more players tie for the most meeples in a completed feature, **all tied players** score the full value (no splitting).

---

## Game End Scoring

When the last tile is placed, score all **incomplete features**:
- Incomplete city: 1 pt per tile, +1 per pennant
- Incomplete road: 1 pt per tile
- Incomplete monastery: 1 pt for the monastery + 1 pt per surrounding tile
- Farms: 3 pts per completed adjacent city (majority farmer scores)

---

## Winning

Player with the highest score wins. Tiebreaker: the tied player who scored more points from completed cities during the game wins.
