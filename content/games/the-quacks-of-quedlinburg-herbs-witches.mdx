## Overview

The Herb Witches is an expansion for The Quacks of Quedlinburg. It introduces new books for ingredients already in the base game, the Herb Witch as a vendor post with new items and abilities, and the Locoweed (white ingredient variant) which mitigates the bag-explosion risk. Requires the base game.

---

## New Components

**New Ingredient Books:** Alternative purchasing rules for Pumpkins, Rubies, Scorpion Tails, and more — giving each ingredient a second scaling book alongside the originals.

**Herb Witch:** A new vendor space on the board. On your turn, you may visit the Herb Witch instead of the Fortune Teller — purchase special herbs and potions with unique effects that alter drawing, scoring, or ingredient management.

**Locoweed (White ingredient):** The Locoweed chip counts as a white chip for explosion purposes but has positive effects when drawn — healing, bonus flasks, or protection. Adding Locoweed chips to your bag counterbalances Cherry Bomb risk.

---

## Rules Changes

At game setup, shuffle two new book cards for each ingredient type (if using The Herb Witches books) and select randomly. This increases variety between sessions.

The Herb Witch sells items for Rubies on your vendor turn — choose between the Fortune Teller and Herb Witch each round.

All other base game rules remain identical.

---

## Winning

Same as the base game: after 9 rounds, player with the most gold coins wins. The Herb Witches components integrate without changing the winning condition.

---

## Tips

- The Locoweed is ideal for aggressive drawers who frequently explode.
- The Herb Witch competes with the Fortune Teller for Ruby spending — plan your Ruby income accordingly.
- New ingredient books can significantly shift the value curves of familiar chips.
