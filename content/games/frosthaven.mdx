## Overview

Frosthaven is a legacy-style cooperative campaign game. Between scenarios, players manage the town of Frosthaven: construct buildings, craft items, manage food and gold reserves, and defend against seasonal raids. In scenarios, each character uses a hand of ability cards to fight monsters, complete objectives, and earn loot.

---

## Setup

Select a scenario from the sticker book. Set up the map tiles, monster standees, and room tiles as shown. Each player picks a character class and takes their character mat, ability cards, and attack modifier deck. Set the round track and threat level as specified.

---

## Scenario Gameplay

**Each round has three phases:**

### 1. Card Selection
Each player secretly selects 2 ability cards from their hand to play this round. The top card determines initiative (lower = earlier).

### 2. Action Phase
Characters and monsters act in initiative order.

Each player plays their 2 cards, choosing one as the **top action** and one as the **bottom action**. Cards offer moves, attacks, heals, summons, and special abilities.

**Monsters** activate automatically per their AI cards: move toward characters, attack if in range, apply conditions.

**Conditions** (Poison, Wound, Immobilize, Stun, etc.) modify normal actions.

**Elements:** Some abilities generate fire, ice, air, earth, light, or dark — others consume them for enhanced effects. Elements persist one round.

### 3. End of Round
Recover cards if needed (lose a turn), refresh items, advance the round track. Some characters have persistent bonuses tracked on their mats.

**Long Rest:** Instead of playing cards, a player may long rest — recover all cards, refresh all items, and heal 2 HP — but lose 1 card permanently and act last this round.

**Short Rest:** During end of round, recover all discard cards except one chosen at random (lost permanently).

---

## Between Scenarios (Outpost Phase)

After each scenario, players:
1. Resolve seasonal events (drawn from the event deck).
2. Build or upgrade town buildings using collected lumber, metal, and hide.
3. Purchase items at unlocked shops.
4. Level up characters (gain new ability cards) if XP thresholds are met.
5. Assign characters to buildings for bonus effects.

---

## Winning

Complete the scenario objective before all characters are exhausted (hand empty or HP at zero). Campaign progress is permanent — stickers applied, buildings built, and story choices recorded.

---

## Tips

- Coordinate initiative to focus fire on dangerous monsters first.
- Element generation and consumption chains are powerful — plan turns ahead.
- Building the town early unlocks crafting and item shops that make later scenarios more manageable.
