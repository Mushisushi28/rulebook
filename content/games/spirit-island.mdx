## Setup

Set up the island board sections (one per player). Place Invader buildings, explorer, and town pieces in the supply. Each spirit takes their unique Presence tokens and starting Power cards. Deal the Invader deck. Set up Fear and Blight cards per difficulty.

---

## Spirit Powers

Each spirit has a unique power card deck and special ability. Powers are played using energy and have ranges and targeting rules. Some spirits are fast (act before Invaders), others slow. Spirits grow each round by taking one of three growth options printed on their board, gaining presence, energy, or card plays.

---

## Turn Structure

**1. Spirit Phase:** Each spirit simultaneously chooses a growth option and selects power cards to play (paying their energy cost).

**2. Fast Power Phase:** Resolve all fast powers in any order.

**3. Invader Phase (three steps):**
- Advance Invader cards: new Explore, Build, and Ravage columns reveal.
- Ravage: Invaders attack every land shown on the Ravage card, dealing damage to Dahan and adding Blight if total damage exceeds land Blight threshold.
- Build: Invaders build towns or cities in all lands shown.
- Explore: New explorers land in coastal/source-adjacent lands.

**4. Slow Power Phase:** Resolve slow powers.

**5. Time Passes:** Clear fear cards, recharge cards, advance terror level.

---

## Winning

Spirits win by meeting the current Terror Level victory condition (drive Invaders to a certain degree) or destroying all Invader presence on the island. Spirits lose if Blight accumulates past the island limit, all spirits are destroyed, or the Invader deck runs out without victory.

---

## Tips

- Coordinate spirits: some excel at pushing Invaders, others at protecting Dahan, others at removing Blight.
- Prioritize lands about to Ravage over Explore phase intervention.
- Fear cards provide powerful one-time effects when earned; build Fear actively.
