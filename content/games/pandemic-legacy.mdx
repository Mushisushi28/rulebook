## Setup

Set up the board as in standard Pandemic. Refer to the Legacy deck and open only the dossier indicated for the current month/game. Apply any stickers or rule changes before beginning the scenario.

---

## Core Rules (Pandemic Base)

Players take 4 actions per turn: move, fly, build a Research Station, share knowledge, treat disease, find cure. Draw 2 Player cards. Epidemic cards trigger outbreaks. The game ends when players cure all 4 diseases (win) or run out of Player cards, place the final Outbreak cube, or exhaust a disease cube supply (lose).

---

## Legacy Additions

**Permanent consequences:** After each game, apply changes to the board, cards, and characters based on the outcome. Cities that outbreak repeatedly gain scars. Characters can gain scars that impose penalties -- too many scars and a character retires permanently.

**Upgrades:** Winning games unlocks new equipment cards, character upgrades, and story events. Players open Legacy Boxes and sealed city dossiers as directed.

**The Story:** Each month represents a new chapter with unique objectives beyond just curing diseases. Missions may require protecting key cities, racing to specific locations, or responding to named viral threats.

---

## Winning the Campaign

Each month is played up to twice (if you lose the first attempt, try again). Both results are recorded. After 12 months, tally the final score based on outcomes. The overall campaign has a narrative ending regardless of performance.

---

## Tips

- Do not sacrifice long-term character health for short-term gains; a scarred character is a liability for the rest of the campaign.
- Read each Legacy card and dossier carefully before opening sealed packets.
- Discuss permanent decisions as a group -- they cannot be undone.
