## Overview

Coup: Rebellion G54 is a standalone bluffing game set in the same universe as Coup. Like Coup, you are trying to eliminate all other players by removing their influence cards. Unlike Coup, the roles available change every game from a pool of 25, so no two sessions play the same way.

---

## Setup

Shuffle all 25 role cards. Randomly select 5 role cards for this game and reveal them to all players. For each selected role, place 3 copies of that role in the game deck (15 cards total). Shuffle and deal 2 cards face-down to each player. These are your hidden roles. Players also start with 2 coins from the supply.

---

## Gameplay

On your turn, take one of the following actions:

**Income:** Take 1 coin from the treasury.

**Foreign Aid:** Take 2 coins. Any player may block Foreign Aid by claiming the Duke role (if Duke is in this game).

**Coup:** Pay 7 coins and eliminate one influence card from any player. This cannot be blocked or challenged.

**Role Actions:** Each of the 5 roles in play has a unique ability (e.g., Ambassador — swap cards with the court deck; Assassin — pay 3 coins to remove a card; Captain — steal 2 coins). Any player may claim any role to use its action, whether or not they actually hold it.

**Challenging:** Any player may challenge a claim by saying "I challenge." The challenged player reveals the claimed role. If they have it, the challenger loses an influence; if they do not, the bluffer loses an influence and must discard that card.

**Blocking:** Some roles block specific actions. Any player can claim a blocker role; the acting player may accept the block or challenge it.

---

## Winning

Players are eliminated when both their influence cards are removed. The last player standing wins.
