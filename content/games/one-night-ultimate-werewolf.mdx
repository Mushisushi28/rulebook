## Setup

Choose a set of role cards (at least one Werewolf and a mix of village roles). Deal one card face-down to each player and place 3 extra cards in the center. All players look at their role in secret.

---

## The Night Phase

Follow the app narrator (or use the included narration instructions). Each role is called in order; players with that role open their eyes and perform their action silently. Common roles:

- **Werewolf:** Open eyes and see other Werewolves. If alone, may look at one center card.
- **Seer:** Look at one other player's card OR two center cards.
- **Robber:** Swap your card with another player's and look at your new card.
- **Troublemaker:** Swap two other players' cards without looking.
- **Drunk:** Swap your card with a center card without looking at it.
- **Insomniac:** Wake last and look at your current card (may have changed).
- **Minion:** Sees Werewolves; wins with them. Werewolves don't know the Minion.

---

## The Day Phase

All players open their eyes. Discuss and debate for a set time (typically 5 minutes). Players may lie or tell the truth freely. No card reveals.

**Vote:** All players simultaneously point at the player they believe is a Werewolf. The player with the most votes is eliminated. Ties result in all tied players being eliminated.

---

## Winning

- **Village team:** At least one Werewolf is eliminated (even if a villager is also eliminated).
- **Werewolf team:** No Werewolf is eliminated. If there are no Werewolves, the Tanner (if used) wins if they die; otherwise village wins only if they kill the Tanner.
- Special edge cases apply for Tanner, Minion, and Doppelganger roles.

---

## Tips

- The center cards are crucial — a claimed role in the center can expose liars.
- Confirm your role at night start before night actions change it.
- Werewolves should blend in by claiming safe roles like Robber or Seer.
